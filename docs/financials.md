## Revenue streams

|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Booking commissions** | % of GMV or flat fee per booking | Core marketplace revenue |
| **Premium / featured listings** | Placement fees or auction | Supply-rich markets; ROI-tested |
| **Subscriptions** | Host or pro SaaS (calendar, analytics, API) | After product-market fit in segment |
| **B2B / enterprise** | Pilot fees, rev-share with partners | As contracted ([enterprise-sales.md](enterprise-sales.md)) |

---

## Projection principles

1. **Bottom-up** — Start from cities × listings × occupancy × nights × average daily rate × take rate.  
2. **Conservative case** — Show base + upside scenario; don’t only show hockey stick.  
3. **Explicit assumptions** — Document ADR, commission %, churn, CAC in appendix.  
4. **Early stage** — If pre-revenue, show **path to first $** and **unit economics hypothesis**, not fake ARR.
