# Weekly enterprise sales review

**Duration:** 30–45 minutes. **Owner:** sales lead (or founder).

**Inputs:** `/admin/sales-dashboard`, calendar (demos), inbox metrics.

---

## 1. Pipeline status

- Count per **stage** — where is inventory stuck longest?  
- **Follow-ups due** this week (`follow_up_at`).  
- **New leads** vs **closed won/lost**.

---

## 2. Conversion rates (approximate)

Track week-over-week (spreadsheet or notebook if needed):

| Transition | Formula |
|------------|---------|
| Lead → contacted | contacted / new leads |
| Contacted → interested | interested / contacted |
| Interested → demo | demos / interested |
| Demo → negotiation | negotiation / demos |
| Negotiation → won | won / negotiation |

**Ranges vary widely** by segment and brand; your baseline matters more than industry averages.

---

## 3. Activity metrics

- **Outreach sent** (manual count or CRM notes).  
- **Replies**.  
- **Demos held** vs scheduled.  
- **Deal estimate** sum for open pipeline (sanity check, not forecast).

---

## 4. Blockers

- Legal, procurement, missing product proof, competitor lock-in.  
- One **owner** and **date** per blocker.

---

## 5. Improvements

- One **script** tweak.  
- One **target list** refresh (segment or geography).  
- One **product** ask for engineering (if blocking demos).
