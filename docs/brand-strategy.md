# Brand strategy — BNHub + LECIPM

**Positioning:** A **trust-forward** real-estate and short-term ecosystem — transparent pricing, verified supply where promised, and respect for both hosts and guests. We compete on **reliability and clarity**, not hype.

---

## Core pillars

1. **Trust** — Verification and safety are product features, not footnotes.  
2. **Transparency** — Fees, rules, and outcomes stated plainly.  
3. **Value** — Fair economics for hosts; fair totals for guests.  
4. **Local depth** — City-by-city quality over hollow national scale claims.

---

## Messaging guidelines

- **Do:** Use specific proof (verification steps, guarantee scope, host support).  
- **Do:** Acknowledge limits (coverage, geography, regulatory variance).  
- **Don’t:** Claim monopoly, guaranteed returns, or “always cheapest.”  
- **Don’t:** Shame competitors by name in paid ads — contrast on attributes instead.

---

## Voice

- **Professional, warm, concise** — bilingual FR/EN in Canadian markets.  
- **Host-facing:** Partner tone; emphasize control and earnings clarity.  
- **Guest-facing:** Reassurance without overpromising amenities you don’t verify.

---

## Social proof

- Real names and permissions for testimonials.  
- Aggregate stats only when **accurate** and **current** (date-stamp large claims).

---

## Crisis communication

- Single approved comms owner; factual updates; link to policies and remediation.
