# Jurisdiction checklist — Canada / Québec (draft for counsel)

**Status:** **DRAFT** — discussion checklist only.  
**Not legal advice.** **Not** a determination of applicable law. **Does not** satisfy any statutory requirement by itself.

**Official sources:** Statutes, regulations, and guidance change — **verify** current text with qualified counsel.

---

## 1. Governing law

| Topic | Checklist item |
|-------|----------------|
| **Contractual choice of law** | ToS placeholder points to **laws of the Province of Québec** and courts of a specified city — **confirm** this matches corporate seat, user base, and **non-waivable** consumer rules. |
| **Federal vs provincial** | PIPEDA (federal private sector) vs Québec Law 25 — **scope** of which regime applies to which processing activities — **counsel**. |
| **Interprovincial** | Users outside Québec — **conflicts** and **enforcement** — discuss. |

---

## 2. Language requirements (French visibility)

| Topic | Checklist item |
|-------|----------------|
| **Bill 96 (Charter of the French Language)** | Public-facing **commercial** documentation, **contracts of adhesion**, and certain **consumer** interactions may require **French** or **bilingual** treatment — **specialist** Québec counsel required. |
| **Website / app** | If **substantially** targeting Québec consumers, discuss **French** versions of Terms, Privacy, and key flows — see `LEGAL-IMPLEMENTATION-NOTES.md` §7. |
| **Process** | Do **not** assume English-only drafts are sufficient for production in Québec without review. |

---

## 3. Consumer protection

| Topic | Checklist item |
|-------|----------------|
| **Québec Consumer Protection Act** | May affect **contracts**, **renewals**, **unfair terms** — ToS **limitation of liability** and **mandatory** clauses need alignment. |
| **Cooling-off / refunds** | Depends on **type** of good/service — digital services, subscriptions, and **lead fees** — **fact-specific**. |
| **Misleading advertising** | Marketing claims about **AI**, **leads**, and **results** — must be **substantiated**; legal standards differ from “marketing comfort.” |

## 4. Dispute resolution

| Topic | Checklist item |
|-------|----------------|

| **Small claims** | Some disputes may fall in **small claims** jurisdiction — discuss **fee** and **venue** rules. |
| **Online dispute / ODR** | If targeting EU users, additional rules may apply — **confirm** if relevant. |
