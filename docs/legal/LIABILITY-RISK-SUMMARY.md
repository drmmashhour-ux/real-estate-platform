## Purpose

---

## 1. What the operator may be responsible for (topics for counsel — not admissions)

**Illustrative only** — actual responsibility depends on facts, contracts, and law:

- Operating the **platform** and **features** as described in published materials (subject to disclaimers).  
- **Security** and **privacy** safeguards **reasonable** in the circumstances (not “absolute” security).  
- **Vendor** selection and **subprocessor** arrangements under applicable privacy law.  
- **Payment facilitation** through **Stripe** per Stripe’s role — **confirm** allocation in Privacy Policy and merchant agreements.  
- **Content moderation** and **abuse** response **as** described in ToS/AUP (avoid promising human review of all content if untrue).

---

## 2. What the platform typically disclaims or limits (draft themes — verify enforceability)

The **draft** Terms (`TERMS-OF-SERVICE-DRAFT.md`) include:

- **“AS IS” / “AS AVAILABLE”** (§8) — **subject to** mandatory consumer protections.  
- **Limitation of liability** (§9) — **placeholder**; **Québec Civil Code** and consumer law may **restrict** broad exclusions.  
- **User-generated content** — users represent rights to listings; operator may **remove** content under ToS.

**Strong disclaimers often needed** (wording TBD with counsel):

| Topic | Risk theme |
|-------|------------|
| **Lead quality** | Users may equate “lead” with “ready to close” — clarify **no guarantee** of conversion, suitability, or financial capacity. |
| **Property accuracy** | Listings are **user-provided** — operator does not **warrant** accuracy of square footage, price, legality of rental, etc., except as law requires. |
| **Third-party actions** | Other users, payment networks, landlords, brokers — **limited control**; fraud and bad-faith conduct risk. |
| **AI recommendations** | Outputs may be **wrong**, **incomplete**, or **outdated** — not professional advice; **no** substitute for inspections, legal advice, or financing advice. |
