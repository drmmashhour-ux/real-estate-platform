## 1. Purpose

The Parties may disclose **Confidential Information** (defined below) in connection with **[DESCRIBE PURPOSE / PROJECT]**.

## 2. Confidential Information

“**Confidential Information**” means non-public information disclosed by a Party or its representatives, whether oral, written, electronic, or visual, including **[business plans, technical data, customer lists, pricing, roadmaps, etc.]**, marked or identified as confidential **or** that reasonably should be understood as confidential.

**Exclusions:** Information that the receiving Party can show (i) was public without breach; (ii) was rightfully known before disclosure; (iii) was independently developed; or (iv) was rightfully received from a third party without restriction—**subject to counsel drafting**.

## 3. Obligations

Each Party shall:

- Use Confidential Information **only** for the **Purpose**;
- Not disclose it to third parties except **representatives** who need to know and are bound to similar duties;
- Protect it using **reasonable** measures (at least the same as for its own similar information).

## 4. Required disclosure

If legally compelled to disclose, the Party shall **notify** the other (if lawful) and cooperate to seek protective treatment—**placeholder for counsel**.
