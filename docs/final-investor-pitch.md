## Full pitch (spoken, first person)

---

### 1. Hook

Hi, I’m [name]. [pause]

I’m building **BNHub** and **LECIPM**. [emphasize]

**One platform** ties **short-term rentals** to **real-estate operations** — so guests, hosts, and professionals are not stuck in **separate tools** that **don’t share trust or data**. [pause]

That’s the opportunity.

---

### 2. Problem

The market is **split**. [emphasize]

**Listings** sit in one place. **Bookings** in another. **Brokers and managers** live in CRMs and email. **Guests** see **partial** fees or **slow** answers. **Hosts** copy the same data into three systems. [pause]

**Trust** and **speed** don’t scale when **nothing connects**. [emphasize]

You get **errors**, **disputes**, and **wasted time** — not because people refuse to pay, but because the **stack is fragmented**. [pause]

---

### 3. Solution

We fix that with **one backbone**. [emphasize]

**LECIPM** is the **real-estate side** — listings, professionals, owner workflows. **BNHub** is the **rental side** — discover, book, run the stay. [pause]

Where we’ve built it, they **share identity, ops, and clarity** — **fewer handoffs**, **clearer** pricing, **one** place to run the business. [pause]

We are **not** promising every feature every giant has on day one. We **are** promising **integration** and **execution** where it **hurts** today. [emphasize]

---

### 4. Product

**BNHub** is the **short-term rental platform** — search, listing pages, booking flow, host tools like calendar and listings. [pause]

**LECIPM** is the **real-estate ecosystem** — broker and owner surfaces, marketplace depth, the **professional layer** around the asset. [pause]

I’ll tell you **exactly** what is **live**, what is **pilot**, and what is **roadmap**. [emphasize] **Milestones** beat **slides full of vapor**. [pause]

---

### 5. Why now

**Demand** for **flexible stays** is **structural** — travel patterns changed; people expect to **book** like they shop online. [pause]

At the same time, **operators** need **better tools** — not more tabs. **Regulation** and **guest expectations** push **verification** and **transparency** up the stack. [pause]

The **window** is **now** because **supply** is **local** and **relationship-driven** — and **software** can finally **connect** the **rental layer** to how **real estate** actually **works**. [emphasize]

---

### 6. Business model

We make money in **two** clear ways. [pause]

**Booking commissions** when a guest **books** on **BNHub**. **Premium features** for **hosts** and **pros** who need **more** — automation, visibility, tools that **save time** or **lift revenue**. [pause]

We may add **featured placement** when the **math** works. **Forward numbers** are **estimates** — labeled in **[financials.md](financials.md)** — not **audited** results. [pause]

---

### 7. Go-to-market

We **start local** — **Montreal** first. [emphasize]

**Why Montreal:** density, bilingual market, **we can stand next to** hosts and **agencies** while we **tighten** the product. [pause]

**How:** **direct onboarding** — I talk to **hosts** and **operators** myself. **Content** that **explains** the product and **builds trust**. **Referrals** so happy users **bring** supply and demand — **tracked**, not **spray and pray**. [pause]

When **repeat bookings** and **partner pull** work **here**, we **repeat** the **playbook** — **discipline**, not **random** national spend. [emphasize]

---

### 8. Competitive advantage

**Airbnb** and **large OTAs** own **consumer habit** at **global** scale. **We respect that.** [pause]

We are **different** in **three** practical ways. [emphasize]

**One:** **Integrated** with **real-estate workflows** — brokers and **multi-unit** operators are **first-class**, not an **afterthought**. **Two:** **Transparency** and **trust** built into **our** flows — **fees** and **status** where we **ship** them, **honestly**. **Three:** **Local depth** — **Montreal** **density** and **relationships** we can **defend** before we **claim** the world. [pause]

We **win** where **ops** and **integration** **matter** — not by **pretending** we **out-brand** Airbnb **tomorrow**. [emphasize]

---

### 9. Founder — why me

I’m a **licensed real-estate broker**. [emphasize]

I’ve **sat** in the **mess** — **leads**, **listings**, **clients**, **vendors**, **deadlines**. I **know** where **trust** breaks and where **time** **vanishes**. [pause]

That **ground truth** is **why** **BNHub** and **LECIPM** are **built** as **one system** — not a **slide** concept from **someone** who **never** ran a **closing** or **a** **host** **payout**. [pause]

My **job** is to **pair** that **domain** **edge** with **shipping** — **weekly** progress, **clear** metrics, **no** **story** that **outruns** the **build**. [emphasize]

---

### 10. Vision

Long term, we want **real estate** and **hospitality** on **one** **ecosystem** — **professionals**, **hosts**, and **guests** on **shared rails**. [pause]

**LECIPM** **anchors** the **asset** and the **deal**. **BNHub** **powers** the **stay**. **Together**, they **compound**. [emphasize]

---

### 11. Ask

We’re raising **[amount]** — **[instrument: SAFE / priced round / etc.]**. [pause]

**Use of funds:** **product** — core booking, trust, and **pro** tools. **Growth** — **Montreal** execution, **content**, **partnerships**, **careful** paid tests **only** when **unit** **economics** **allow**. [emphasize]

I want investors who **value** **honest** **updates** and **milestones** we **control**. [pause]

**[15–20 minutes]** — I’ll **walk** you through **the deck** and **the product**. [pause]

Thank you. **Questions?**
