# Platform Governance Framework

Rules and standards for listings, professionals, disputes, fraud, and user behavior. This document protects the platform legally and operationally and should guide moderation and enforcement.

---

## 1. Platform participation rules

- Participation is subject to these rules and to applicable law.
- Users must provide accurate identity and contact information where required.
- Licensed professionals must maintain valid credentials and comply with local regulatory requirements.
- Owners and hosts may list only properties they are authorized to offer (sale or rental).
- Investors must not use the platform for misrepresentation, market manipulation, or prohibited schemes.
- The platform may restrict or terminate access for violation of these rules or for operational or legal reasons.

---

## 2. Verification standards

- **Identity**: The platform may require identity verification for certain actions (e.g. listing, investing, professional services). Standards and methods are defined in internal procedures and reflected in the [trust framework](PLATFORM-MISSION.md#trust-and-verification-framework).
- **Professionals**: Brokers and agents must complete professional verification (e.g. license checks) before being represented as “verified” or “licensed” on the platform.
- **Properties**: Listings may be subject to ownership or right-to-list verification as defined in product and operational policies.
- Verification status may be displayed to users to support trust; removal or expiry of verification will be handled according to internal processes.

---

## 3. Fraud and scam prevention

- Listing fraud (e.g. fake properties, misleading photos or descriptions, phantom listings) is prohibited and may result in removal and account action.
- Financial fraud (e.g. fake payments, investment scams, phishing) is prohibited; the platform will cooperate with law enforcement where appropriate.
- Identity and credential fraud (e.g. impersonation, fake licenses) is prohibited and will result in permanent restriction where confirmed.
- The platform uses proactive detection (signals, reports, patterns) and reactive measures (investigations, blocks, referrals) to reduce fraud. Specific controls are documented in internal security and trust operations.

---

## 4. Professional conduct requirements

- Licensed professionals must comply with applicable professional and ethical rules (e.g. real estate board standards, advertising rules).
- Communication with users must be honest, non-discriminatory, and free from harassment or pressure.
- Fees, commissions, and incentives must be disclosed where required by product or law.
- Misrepresentation of credentials, listings, or outcomes is prohibited and may lead to loss of verification and access.

---

## 5. Dispute resolution system

- Users, professionals, owners, and investors can report issues (e.g. listing accuracy, conduct, payments) through designated channels.
- The platform will triage reports, gather information, and attempt resolution in line with these rules and applicable terms of use.
- Outcomes may include: no action, warning, listing removal, restriction of features, account suspension, or permanent ban. Serious or illegal activity may be referred to authorities.
- Dispute resolution processes and timelines are documented in internal operations; users may be informed of outcomes where appropriate.

---

## 6. Platform enforcement actions

- **Warnings**: Notice to the participant to correct behavior or content.
- **Content removal**: Removal of specific listings, messages, or other content.
- **Feature restriction**: Temporary or permanent restriction of certain features (e.g. listing, messaging, verification badge).
- **Account suspension**: Temporary loss of access, with conditions for reinstatement where applicable.
- **Permanent ban**: Permanent loss of access and, where appropriate, exclusion from future use.
- **Legal referral**: Reporting to regulators or law enforcement when required or appropriate.

Enforcement is applied consistently with these rules, internal procedures, and the [platform mission and trust framework](PLATFORM-MISSION.md). Severity and repeat offenses are taken into account.

---
