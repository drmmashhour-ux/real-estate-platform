# Operations — BNHub + LECIPM (enterprise)

**Scope:** Support, moderation, escalation, and baseline **SLAs** for a growing marketplace. Tune numbers to team size — the structure matters more than the exact hours.

---

## Support workflows

| Tier | Channel | Intake |
|------|---------|--------|
| L1 | Email / chat / form | Triage, macros, refunds within policy |
| L2 | Specialist | Payments, trust, complex disputes |
| L3 | Leadership / legal | Regulatory, PR-risk, law enforcement |

**Tools:** Link tickets to `userId`, `bookingId`, `listingId` for auditability.

---

## Moderation

- **Listings:** policy violations, discrimination, misleading photos.  
- **Reviews:** fraud and retaliation patterns.  
- **Messaging:** safety escalations only per published policy.

Actions: warn → limit visibility → suspend → ban (documented criteria).

---

## Escalation handling

- **Safety imminent** — immediate human review; preserve evidence.  
- **Payment failure** — finance + support joint playbook.  
- **Partner incident** — account manager + trust lead.

---

## SLAs (starting targets)

| Case type | First response | Resolution target |
|-----------|----------------|-------------------|
| Booking blocked / payment | &lt; 4 business hours | &lt; 24 hours |
| General guest/host | &lt; 24 hours | &lt; 72 hours |
| Dispute opened | &lt; 24 hours | Per policy timeline |
| Trust / safety urgent | &lt; 1 hour | Same day |

Publish public-facing SLAs only if you can meet them.

---

## Handoffs with product/engineering

- Weekly top 10 ticket themes → backlog.  
- Severity-1 incidents → postmortem within 5 business days.
