## 1. BNHub concept

BNHub is a **short-term rental marketplace** integrated inside the LECIPM ecosystem.

Unlike traditional rental platforms, BNHub connects:

- **Property owners**
- **Licensed real-estate brokers**
- **Travelers / tenants**
- **Investors**

inside **one verified property identity system** linked to cadastral verification and trust mechanisms.

### Main goals

- **Reduce fraud** in rental listings
- **Integrate real-estate professionals** (brokers in the flow)
- **Connect rentals with investment opportunities**
- **Provide AI-driven property discovery**

### Positioning

> **“A verified short-term rental network integrated with a professional real-estate ecosystem.”**

**Key differentiation** vs. Airbnb / Booking.com:

| Differentiator | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| Verified property identity | Linked to cadastral records; reduces fake listings. |
| Broker integration | Real estate professionals participate directly; most rental platforms exclude brokers. |
| Investment integration | Users can move from rent → purchase → investment in the same ecosystem. |
| AI-driven marketplace | Pricing, discovery, recommendations via AI Control Center. |

## 2. BNHub revenue streams

### 2.1 Guest service fee

A fee charged to guests at booking.

| Item | Range / value |
|------|----------------|
| **Typical range** | 8% – 15% of booking price |
| **Example** | Night price $200 → Guest fee (12%) $24 → **Total paid by guest $224** |

*(Source: Airbnb Help Center – service fees documentation; similar ranges used by short-term rental platforms.)*

### 2.2 Host commission

Hosts pay a commission on each reservation.

| Item | Range / value |
|------|----------------|
| **Typical range** | 3% – 10% |
| **Example** | Booking price $200 → Host commission (5%) $10 → **Host receives $190** |

This dual-fee system (guest + host) is a common marketplace model used by large travel platforms.

### 2.3 Listing promotion fees

Hosts or brokers can boost visibility of listings.

| Promotion | Price |
|-----------|--------|
| Featured listing | $15 – $50 |
| Homepage spotlight | $50 – $150 |
| Search priority placement | Dynamic auction |

Sponsored listings are widely used in digital marketplaces.

### 2.4 Broker subscription (professional tools)

Brokers get access to CRM, analytics, automation, and multi-property management.

| Plan | Price |
|------|--------|
| Starter | $29 / month |
| Professional | $79 / month |
| Agency | $199 / month |

Typical SaaS subscription model for CRM systems.

### 2.5 Cleaning & property services marketplace

BNHub can monetize ancillary services:

- Cleaning services
- Property management
- Maintenance
- Concierge services

**Platform commission:** 10% – 20%.

### 2.6 Payment processing fee

BNHub may include a payment processing margin.

**Typical range:** 2% – 3% + fixed fee (e.g. payment provider pricing such as Stripe).

## 3. AI monetization layer

LECIPM includes an **AI Control Center**; BNHub can offer paid AI services.

| Service | Description | Example pricing |
|---------|-------------|------------------|
| **AI pricing tools** | Dynamic rent suggestions (demand, events, seasonality) | $10 – $30 / month per property |
| **AI listing optimization** | Improves description, pricing, photo ranking, SEO | $15 / month |
| **AI fraud detection** | Platform-level: duplicate listings, suspicious behavior, payment anomalies | Protects platform reputation; reduces risk |

## 4. Trust & compliance system

BNHub differentiates with **identity verification of properties**.

Listing verification may include:

- **Cadastral number validation**
- **Land registry check**
- **Broker license verification**

This reduces fake listings. Fraud protection is a core feature in large marketplaces (e.g. Airbnb Trust & Safety documentation).

*(Aligned with [Platform Mission — Trust framework](PLATFORM-MISSION.md#trust-and-verification-framework) and [Governance](PLATFORM-GOVERNANCE.md).)*
