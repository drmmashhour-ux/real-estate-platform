# CURSOR ORDER — FILE 1: EXERCISE 4 (SHAPE BUILDER BIRD)

## GOAL

Rebuild bird using:

- Shapes (Ellipse)
- Shape Builder Tool (`Shift + M`)

Do **not** use:

- Raster artwork
- Pen-only final drawing

---

## STEP 1 — SETUP

1. Open Illustrator.
2. Create a new file:
   - 11" x 8.5" (RGB)
3. Open the Layers panel and create:
   - `REFERENCE`
   - `VECTOR`

---

## STEP 2 — PLACE TEMPLATE

1. Select the `REFERENCE` layer.
2. Go to **File -> Place** and select image.
3. Set opacity to **30%**.
4. Lock the layer.

---

## STEP 3 — CREATE BASE SHAPES

1. Go to `VECTOR` layer.
2. Use **Ellipse Tool (L)**.
3. Create:
   - Circle -> body
   - Ellipses -> wings
   - Small circle -> eye

---

## STEP 4 — SHAPE BUILDER (KEY)

1. Select overlapping shapes.
2. Press **Shift + M**.

Then:

- Drag to combine.
- Hold **Alt** and click to remove parts.

---

## STEP 5 — BEAK

1. Use a triangle or ellipse.
2. Use Shape Builder to refine.

---

## STEP 6 — ORBIT LINES

1. Draw circles.
2. Use **Scissors Tool (C)** to cut arcs.

---

## STEP 7 — COLOR

Fill shapes after building.

---

## STEP 8 — CLEAN

Delete `REFERENCE` layer.

---

## STEP 9 — SAVE

Save as:

`Ex_4_YourName.ai`
