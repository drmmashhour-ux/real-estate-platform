# Operator referral script — warm intro to other residences

Use with **existing partners** after you’ve started sending families. Personalize **[Name]** and **[City]**.

---

## Opening

> “Hey [Name], quick question —  
> since we started sending you families, do you know **other residences** that would benefit from this?”

→ **Pause.** Let them think.

---

## Positioning

> “We’re currently onboarding a **small number** of residences in **[City]**, and we’re trying to work with the **right partners**.”

---

## Referral ask (important)

> “If you know **1 or 2 good ones**, I can give them **early access** — **same setup** as you.”

---

## Incentive

> “And for **every referral**, I can send you **priority leads** or **a few free ones**.”

*(Only promise what your economics and product actually allow.)*

---

## Soft close

> “Anyone comes to mind?”

---

## End — actions

1. Aim for **1–3 names** (org + contact if possible).  
2. **DM or call** referred operators **the same day**.  
3. Log referrals in your sheet (source partner · referred residence · date).
