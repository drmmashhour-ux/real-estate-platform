# 100 real family users in 7 days (GTM sprint)

**Goal:** **~100 real people** (families / caregivers) in one week using **direct outreach**, **local targeting**, and **high-intent channels**—not paid blitz.

**Reality check:** 10–20 users/day is **aggressive**; it works if you **respond in minutes** and keep the offer **help-first**. Track signups in a **sheet** (name, source, date, next step, match status).

**Product alignment (LECIPM):**  
- **Hub + match flow:** ` /en/ca/senior-living` (default **en** + **ca**; adjust locale/country if needed).  
- **Results / follow-up:** ` /en/ca/senior-living/results` and residence detail ` /en/ca/senior-living/[id]`.  
- **Day 5 “pages”** can be real **SEO routes** in the app (or landers)—coordinate with `sitemap` / `app` structure so you do not duplicate thin content.

---

## Day 1 — Setup

- [ ] **Landing / path** — Use the **live senior-living entry** (or a **dedicated short link** to the same flow: e.g. `yoursite.com/en/ca/senior-living`).
- [ ] **Form / match flow** — Ensure the **match questionnaire** runs end-to-end on mobile (most traffic will be phone).
- [ ] **Prepare 3–5 real residences** — Names + neighbourhoods + one line each so replies feel concrete (“we often suggest X in Y for budgets like Z”).
- [ ] **Tracking** — Spreadsheet columns: Source · First contact · Landing URL · Completed match? · Referrer name.

---

## Day 2 — Facebook groups (high impact)

**Search (examples):**

- “résidence personnes âgées Montréal”  
- “aider parents logement”  
- “caregiver groups” (+ your city)

**Post (adapt tone to group rules):**

> Hi, I’m helping families find senior residences faster.  
> If anyone is **currently looking**, I can suggest **a few options** based on your situation.

**Then:**

1. Reply to **every comment** publicly (short, kind).  
2. **DM** people who engage — move to WhatsApp/email only if allowed by group admins.

---

## Day 3 — Marketplace / classifieds

**Where:** Facebook Marketplace · Kijiji (and equivalents you trust).

**Title:**

> Helping families find senior residence **(free help)**

**Body:** Plain language — **no hype** — link or “message me” CTA. Follow **each platform’s rules** (avoid spam categories; refresh posts if policy allows).

---

## Day 4 — Direct messages

**Find:**

- Threads where people **ask questions**  
- Families **posting needs**

**DM:**

> Hi — I saw your post. I help match families with residences **based on situation** (budget, area, care needs).  
> If you want, I can suggest **a few options** — no obligation.

Keep it **one screen** max on first touch.

---

## Day 5 — Local SEO quick win

**Targets (examples — align with your metro):**

- “résidence personnes âgées Laval”  
- “senior living Montreal”  

**Implementation options:**

1. **In-app SEO pages** (preferred long-term): city intent pages under your existing `[locale]/[country]` routing.  
2. **Temporary:** one clean **FAQ-style** paragraph + CTA linking to **`/senior-living`** — avoid doorway pages with no unique value.

**Goal:** Indexed later; **immediate** traffic still comes from Days 2–4.

---

## Day 6 — Referral loop

Ask **every** engaged user:

> Do you know **someone else** who’s looking?

Offer (only if you can deliver):

- **Faster** triage / reply  
- **Priority** matching for their referral

Log referral source in your sheet.

---

## Day 7 — Push + follow-up

- [ ] **Follow up all open conversations** — no ghost threads.  
- [ ] **Resend** shortlists or “next step” (call residence, book tour).  
- [ ] **Push to book visits** — offer to **introduce** or **confirm** tour slots if your process allows.

---

## Expected result

| Metric | Stretch target |
|--------|----------------|
| New engaged users / day | **10–20** (varies by city + your speed) |
| Week total | **~100** meaningful contacts (not just clicks) |
| Outcome | **First real leads** into match flow + operators |

---

## Important rules

1. **Human, not salesy** — You’re **helping** someone under stress.  
2. **Fast response** — **&lt; 5 minutes** when possible (notifications on for DMs).  
3. **Help first** — Pitch LECIPM **after** you understand situation; **never** pressure.

---

## Final result

You aim for:

- **Real traffic** (groups, classifieds, DMs, SEO seed)  
- **Real leads** (completed match or handoff)  
- **First conversions** (visit booked or operator loop started)
