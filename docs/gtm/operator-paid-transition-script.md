# Operator script — check-in → transition to pay-per-lead

Use after **free trial leads** when you’re ready to move operators to **paid qualified requests**. Adjust **[Name]** and price band to your live offer.

---

## Opening

> “Hi [Name], I just wanted to check in quickly — **how did the leads we sent you go?**”

→ **Let them talk.**

---

## If they had a visit or interest

> “Perfect — that’s exactly what we aim for.  
>  
> This is where most of our partners start seeing **real value** — when **visits turn into residents**.”

---

## Transition to payment

> “What we usually do next is very simple:  
> we **continue** sending you **qualified families**, and you **only pay per lead**.”

---

## Price introduction (confident)

> “For your area, it’s typically around **$30–$50** per **qualified request**.”

👉 **Pause.** Let silence work.

---

## Value reinforcement

> “To give you perspective — **one resident** covers **months** of marketing cost.  
> So even **one conversion** makes this very profitable.”

---

## Soft close

> “Would you like us to **continue sending you leads** this week?”

---

## If hesitation

> “No problem — we can start with **a few more** and you **only pay if you see value**.  
> The goal is to make this **work for you**.”

---

## Final close

> “I’ll **activate** it on your account so you **keep receiving requests** — and we’ll **track results together**.”

---

## End — actions

1. **Send payment link** or **activate billing** in your system (Stripe / internal).  
2. **Follow up in 48h** — confirm first paid period, answer questions, review lead quality.
