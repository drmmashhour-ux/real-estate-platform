# Outreach copy — families (senior living)

Short, human messages for DMs and comments. Adjust emoji and tone to match the platform.

---

## Direct message (Facebook / Reddit / forums)

```
Hi! I saw your post — I help families find senior residences based on care level, budget, and location.

If you want, I can suggest a few options for your situation (no cost).

Just let me know:
• city
• level of help needed
• approximate budget

I’ll send you a few good matches 🙏
```

**Tips:** Reply fast; replace “your post” with specifics when you can (“the question about…”); drop emoji if the thread is formal.

---

## Variant (even shorter)

```
Hi — saw your message. I help match families with residences (care level, budget, area). Happy to suggest a few options — no fee. Which city are you focusing on?
```

---

## Variant (empathetic open — warm tone)

```
Hi 😊 I came across your post — I know how difficult it can be to find the right place.

I’ve been helping families compare a few good options based on their needs.

If you’d like, I can send you some suggestions — just tell me roughly what you’re looking for.
```

**Tips:** Strong for emotional threads; swap 😊 for nothing on serious/medical-focused groups if it feels too casual.

---

## Follow-up / check-in (after silence or partial reply)

```
Hi again — just wanted to check in 😊

I found a couple of good options that might fit what you’re looking for.

Would you like me to send them to you?
```

**Tips:** Send **Day 2–3** after first touch if they engaged but stalled; keep it opt-in (“would you like”) so it doesn’t feel pushy. Remove emoji for a more neutral follow-up.
