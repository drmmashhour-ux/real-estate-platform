# Positioning snippets (operators & families)

Short lines you can drop into calls, DMs, emails, or landing pages. Tune voice (formal vs warm) per channel.

---

## Exclusive supply / quality (operators)

Use when explaining why you’re **selective** or moving **slowly** in one area.

```
Right now, we’re only working with a small number of residences per area.

The goal is to avoid sending the same families to too many places and keep quality high.

We’re selecting partners carefully.
```

**When to use:** referral conversations, onboarding, objections (“why not everyone?”), waitlists.
