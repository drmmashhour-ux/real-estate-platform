# Operator sales call — script (≈2 min + listen time)

Use on **phone or video** with a residence director / operator. Personalize `[Name]` and `[City]`.

---

## Opening (0–30s)

> “Hi [Name], thanks for taking a few minutes.  
> I’ll be quick — I’m working with a few residences in [City] to send them families **actively looking** for a place right now.”

**Pause.**

---

## Problem (30–60s)

> “Most families today are **lost** — they don’t know where to start, and operators rely on **outdated listings** or **word-of-mouth**.”

---

## Solution (60–90s)

> “We built a system that **matches** families based on their needs — care level, budget, location — and sends **qualified visit requests** directly to you.”

---

## Validation (90–120s)

> “We’re already **onboarding a few residences** and starting to **generate requests** — I’m **selecting a small group** to test this with.”

---

## Engagement question (important)

> “Right now, **how do most families usually find you?**”

- Let them talk.  
- Listen carefully.  
- Use their language in the next beat.

---

## Positioning (based on their answer)

> “I see — so what we’re doing **complements** that by bringing in families who are **already ready to visit**, not just browsing.”

---

## Offer (key moment)

> “For **early partners**, we’re offering:  
> - **free listing**  
> - **first leads free**  
> - **no commitment**  
>  
> Just to **prove the value**.”

---

## Close (soft)

> “What I suggest is simple — we **set you up**, send you **a few requests**, and **if it works**, we continue.”

---

## Objection handling

**“I don’t have time.”**  
→ “Totally understand — setup takes **5–10 minutes**, **we handle everything**.”

**“I already have leads.”**  
→ “That’s perfect — this just adds an **extra channel**, **no risk**.”

**“I’m not sure.”**  
→ “That’s exactly why we start with **free leads** — you **only continue if it works**.”

---

## Final close

> “Can we get you **onboarded today** so you can start receiving requests **this week**?”

---

## End

1. **Send onboarding link immediately** (same call if possible).  
2. **Follow up same day** — short confirmation + link again if needed.

---

## Notes for the caller

- Keep Problem + Solution tight; **win on the engagement question**.  
- Never argue — **mirror**, then bridge to complementarity.  
- After “yes,” **time-box** onboarding (“today / this week”) so momentum holds.
