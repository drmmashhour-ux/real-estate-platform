## Follow-up messages (turn matches into visits)

### After sending matches

> I found a few options that could fit your situation 🙏  
>  
> The **first one** is usually a good match based on what you told me.  
>  
> Would you like me to help you **arrange a visit**?

### Push to action

> Most families find it much easier **after seeing the place in person**.  
>  
> I can help you **schedule a visit** — it’s usually the best next step 🙂

### Gentle urgency

> Just a heads-up — places in this range **tend to go quickly**.  
>  
> If one looks interesting, I recommend **visiting soon**.

### If hesitant

> Totally understand — it’s a **big decision**.  
>  
> If it helps, we can just start with **one visit**, no pressure at all.

## High-conversion flow (use this order)

1. **DM** → start conversation  
2. Ask **3 questions** (city / care / budget)  
3. Send **3 options**  
4. **Highlight 1** as “best match”  
5. **Push for visit**  

---

## Psychology (why this works)

- You’re **helping**, not selling  
- You **reduce overwhelm**  
- You **guide** the decision  
- You **build trust**
