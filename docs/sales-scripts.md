# Enterprise sales scripts — BNHub + LECIPM

**Use:** Personalize `{contact}`, `{company}`, `{city}` before sending. **Do not** promise guaranteed bookings or revenue.

---

## Initial outreach

> Hi, I'm reaching out because we're launching BNHub, a platform designed to help property managers increase visibility and bookings while simplifying operations.
>
> We're onboarding a select group of partners and would like to explore if it could fit your portfolio.

**Variant (agency):** Swap “property managers” for “real estate teams adding short-term revenue streams.”

---

## Follow-up

> Just following up — we're currently prioritizing early partners for additional exposure and support.

**If no reply after second touch:** Short single line + specific ask: “Worth a 15-min call next Tuesday or Thursday?”

---

## Demo script (practical)

1. **Context (2 min)** — Their portfolio size, cities, current tools.  
2. **Platform (5 min)** — Guest search → listing detail → fee transparency.  
3. **Listing visibility (3 min)** — Where listings appear; any campaigns or featured placements *you actually run*.  
4. **Booking flow (5 min)** — Request/book path; host notifications; payment handoff as implemented.  
5. **Host / partner view (5 min)** — Dashboard, calendar, pricing — show what exists today.  
6. **Analytics (3 min)** — Reports available; define what you will share monthly in pilot.  
7. **Q&A (5 min)** — Support, verification, disputes — point to policies.

**Close demo:** “Based on what you saw, does a small pilot with {N} listings make sense to measure performance together?”

---

## Closing script

> Based on what we discussed, we can onboard your listings and start testing performance with no heavy commitment upfront.
>
> Would you like to start with a small set of properties?

**If hesitation:** “We can start with {N} units for {timebox} and review numbers together — no long-term lock-in at this stage.”

---

## Objection snippets

| Objection | Response direction |
|-----------|-------------------|
| “We already use OTA X” | “Many partners use both; we focus on direct/brand and tools in one place.” |
| “Fees?” | Share current fee table; compare to their fully loaded cost on OTAs. |
| “Compliance?” | High-level only; escalate detailed legal to counsel. |
| “Volume guarantee?” | “We don’t guarantee volume; we’ll align on pilot metrics and optimize together.” |
