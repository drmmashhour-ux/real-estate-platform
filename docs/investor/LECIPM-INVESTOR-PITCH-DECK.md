## Phase 2 — Problem

**Slide title:** Stays and rentals still break down for guests and hosts

**Guest pain**

- **Opaque totals** — fees and policies appear late; trust erodes before payment.
- **Fragmented status** — payment, confirmation, and instructions live in email and DMs.
- **Weak local depth** — global brands optimize for scale, not your city’s supply quality.

**Host and operator challenges**

- **Ops overhead** — calendars, payouts, and guest messaging do not compound on one stack.
- **Yield pressure** — hard to merchandise add-ons and protection without looking predatory.
- **Professional gap** — serious hosts and small operators need tools closer to **real estate** workflows than a consumer-only app.

**One-line close**  
The market rewards **clarity**, **verified flows**, and **integrated** booking plus operations — not another me-too listing grid.

---

## Phase 3 — Solution

**Slide title:** LECIPM — search, book, and operate with trust built in

**Platform pillars**

- **AI-assisted discovery** — smarter search and merchandising on city and intent pages *(ship vs roadmap labeled honestly on the slide footnote)*.
- **End-to-end booking** — quotes, Stripe Checkout, webhook-confirmed status, mobile deep links back to the app.
- **Trust features** — clear payment state, host guidance after confirmation, insurance and partner flows where appropriate, admin and moderation hooks for quality.

**Positioning line**  
We combine **guest experience**, **host economics**, and **platform-grade** payments and analytics so growth compounds.

## Phase 6 — Business model

**Slide title:** Multiple aligned revenue streams

**Core**

- **Booking-related fees** — service fee on guest checkout *(transparent line items when itemized checkout is on)*; aligns platform revenue with **completed stays**.

**High-margin extensions**

- **Insurance and protection leads** — optional, consent-based; partner fulfillment; tracked funnel from listing, checkout, and post-confirmation moments.

- **Upsells at checkout** — early check-in, late check-out, bundled peace-of-mind SKUs — **attachment rate** and **AOV lift** measurable in product analytics.

**Optional (roadmap / where live)**

- **Featured placement and host promotions** when ROI-positive for supply.
- **B2B / pro** tools and referrals tied to the broader LECIPM marketplace.
