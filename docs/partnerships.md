# Partnerships — BNHub + LECIPM

Partners extend **supply**, **distribution**, and **trust** without diluting brand standards. Each type has a lightweight onboarding path and clear integration boundaries.

**B2B pipeline & CRM:** [enterprise-sales.md](enterprise-sales.md) and `/admin/sales-dashboard`.

---

## Partnership types

### 1. Property managers / agencies

**Goal:** Bulk quality supply, professional calendars, fewer no-shows.  
**Onboarding:** commercial terms, brand use, listing quality SLA, single point of contact.  
**Integration:** bulk listing tools or API (when available); reporting dashboard; `source=partner` in CRM.

### 2. Travel-adjacent services

**Goal:** Transfers, experiences, insurance — improve trip completion rate.  
**Onboarding:** legal review, revenue share, data sharing minimization.  
**Integration:** deep links, post-booking offers, **no** silent data resale.

### 3. Marketing partners

**Goal:** Co-marketing in specific cities (influencers, local media).  
**Onboarding:** disclosure rules, UTM discipline, creative approval.  
**Integration:** landing pages + tracked campaigns; weekly performance review.

### 4. Enterprise clients

**Goal:** Relocation, corporate housing, developer rentals.  
**Onboarding:** MSA, invoicing, duty of care, support SLAs.  
**Integration:** dedicated reporting, optional SSO (future); billing separation.

---

## Integration guidelines

- **APIs:** Versioned JSON; rate limits; sandbox keys for partners.  
- **Data:** least privilege; audit partner access.  
- **Brand:** co-branding guidelines in [brand-strategy.md](brand-strategy.md).  
- **Exit:** data export and wind-down clause in agreements.

---

## Success metrics

- Net new **published** listings (quality-weighted).  
- Bookings and **GMV** attributed to partner (UTM + internal tags).  
- Dispute/chargeback rate vs baseline — pause partners that degrade trust.
