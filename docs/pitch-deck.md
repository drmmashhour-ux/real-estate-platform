## Premium visual system (quick reference)

| Element | Spec |
|---------|------|
| Background | `#0B0B0B` |
| Accent | `#D4AF37` |
| Panels / charts | `#1A1A1A` |
| Soft highlight | `#E6C76E` (sparingly) |
| Body text | `#FFFFFF` / `#BFBFBF` |

**Principles:** Minimal copy, **one** focal point per slide, **thin-line** gold or white icons, **no** busy backgrounds. Full tokens, type sizes, components → [design-system.md](design-system.md).

## Slide 1 — Title

**On-slide copy (minimal)**

- **BNHub** + **LECIPM** (lockup or stacked; **BNHub** may carry **gold** wordmark per brand guidelines)
- *Integrated real estate & short-term stays*
- One line: **Trusted discovery, booking, and professional workflows — together.**
- **[Company]** · **[Founder]** — **Confidential** · [Date]

**Premium design**

- **Layout A:** Vertically centered block; **60%+** empty space.
- **Typography:** Title **48–56 pt** white; tagline **18–20 pt** `#BFBFBF`; footer **12–14 pt** muted.
- **Accent:** Thin **gold** horizontal rule **80–120 pt** under main title **or** gold **dot** flanking tagline.
- **No** imagery except logo — **pure** `#0B0B0B` or **subtle** gradient per design-system.

## Slide 2 — Problem

**Fragmentation erodes trust and scale**

- Real estate and STR tools **don’t talk** — siloed CRM, listings, payouts, support.
- **Guests:** uneven verification and fee clarity across channels.
- **Hosts & operators:** manual ops; hard to prove quality at scale.
- **Professionals:** time lost to handoffs, not closing.

*Footer (muted):* Point solutions don’t compound — **integrated trust + operations** do.

**Premium design**

- **Layout B:** Left: title + **4** bullets max. Right: **diagram** — three **#1A1A1A** rounded rectangles labeled *Listings / Bookings / Ops* with **broken** gold connectors; **thin-line** icons (lock, calendar, chat) in gold.
- **Emphasis:** Pull **one** phrase into **gold** in the title, e.g. **trust**.

---

## Slide 3 — Solution

**One ecosystem: property + stays**

- **LECIPM** — Real-estate marketplace, professional tools, investor surfaces (as live).
- **BNHub** — STR: listings, booking, payments narrative, host/guest flows.
- Shared where built: **identity, verification posture, admin ops, data layer.**

*Footer:* We win on **depth** in priority markets — not replacing every global incumbent day one.

**Premium design**

- **Layout B:** Right: **two** nodes **LECIPM** | **BNHub** on `#1A1A1A` cards, **single gold line** or bridge between; optional small **“shared layer”** bar in `#E6C76E` at **low opacity**.
- Icons: **building** + **key** (thin stroke gold).
