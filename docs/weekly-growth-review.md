# Weekly growth review — 10K scaling

**When:** same slot every week (60–90 min). **Who:** growth + supply lead + (optional) product.

**Inputs:** `/admin/growth-dashboard`, `/admin/growth-crm`, ad manager exports, support themes.

---

## 1. What worked

- **Metric:** e.g. signups +X%, bookings +Y, CAC −Z%.  
- **Channel:** e.g. Meta host creative, DM blitz in City A.  
- **Product:** e.g. checkout drop reduced after copy change.

*Write 3 bullets max — specific.*

---

## 2. What to scale

- **Budget:** +10–20% on winning ad sets ([ads-scaling.md](ads-scaling.md)).  
- **Content:** double down on top 2 video themes from CTR / saves.  
- **Outbound:** assign more capacity to `HIGH` tier CRM + under-supplied cities from dashboard.

---

## 3. What to stop

- Ads in bottom 30% by CPA or signup rate.  
- Channels with repeated low reply rate (unless strategic).  
- Features or pages with high drop and no owner — park or fix.

---

## 4. Marketplace balance

- List **under-supplied cities** (from dashboard) → host sprint goals for the week.  
- List **liquid cities** → guest campaigns and retargeting.

---

## 5. Retention & referrals

- Dormant segments emailed? (use `services/growth/retention.ts` copy.)  
- Top referrers from leaderboard — who gets visibility boost this week?

---

## 6. Next week’s bets

| Bet | Owner | Success metric |
|-----|--------|----------------|
| e.g. New host LP + 3 creatives | Name | Host signups / approved listings |
| e.g. Retargeting 7-day visitors | Name | Checkout starts |

---

## 7. Risks / blockers

- Legal, trust & safety, payment incidents, key hire gaps.
