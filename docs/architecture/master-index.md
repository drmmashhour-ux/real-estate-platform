# LECIPM Engineering Blueprint — Master Index

**Central strategy reference:** [Master Strategy Book](../MASTER-STRATEGY-BOOK.md) — 16-chapter strategic manual compiling vision, architecture, modules, trust, defense, AI, business model, growth, roadmap, launch, scaling, operations, founder strategy, and long-term vision. **One-page overview:** [War Map](../WAR-MAP.md) — Single strategic overview showing how every major part of the company fits together; for leadership at a glance.

This index links to all major architecture and implementation documents and suggests the order they should be read for efficient onboarding and build execution.
