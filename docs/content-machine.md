# Content machine — daily execution (10K scale)

**Output target**

- **3–5 short videos** per day (repurpose one master cut per topic across TikTok, Reels, Facebook Reels).
- **1–2 feed posts** (carousel, photo, or text thread) for trust and SEO-adjacent engagement.
- **Storytelling + value:** every asset answers “who is this for?” in the first second.

---

## Daily rhythm

| Block | Duration | Output |
|-------|----------|--------|
| Morning | 45 min | Script 3 videos from `buildDailyAutopilotBrief().contentIdeas` or [content-30days.md](content-30days.md) rotation |
| Midday | 30 min | Film batch (phone + lav mic is enough) |
| Afternoon | 45 min | Edit captions, covers, UTM links in bio |
| Evening | 15 min | Reply to comments; pin best question |

---

## Pillars (balance weekly)

1. **Find a stay** — search demo, filters, price clarity.  
2. **Earn as host** — fees, onboarding, calendar tips.  
3. **Trust** — verification, safety, real quotes.  
4. **Local** — city-specific demand and events.  
5. **Social proof** — guests + hosts (permissioned).

---

## Distribution

- **TikTok** — broad discovery; hook-heavy.  
- **Instagram** — Reels + Stories for retargeting warm followers.  
- **Facebook** — Reels + groups (where allowed); host angle often outperforms.

---

## Measurement

- Track **profile link clicks** and **landing visits** by UTM per platform weekly.  
- Tie winning topics back to **bookings or signups** in CRM `source` (`tiktok`, `instagram`, etc.).

---

## Tools in repo

- `services/growth/content-engine.ts` — rotating ideas.  
- `services/growth/ai-autopilot.ts` — bundles daily brief with outreach + ads.  
- [content-30days.md](content-30days.md) — full topic list.

---

## Quality bar (avoid noise)

- One idea per video.  
- On-screen text for silent viewers.  
- CTA: single action (link in bio, comment HOST, save).
