# City launch playbook — BNHub + LECIPM

Repeatable steps for **entering or deepening** a metro. Skip nothing that affects trust or legal readiness.

---

## 1. Validate demand

- Search interest (SEO/PPC tests), waitlist, inbound partner leads.  
- **Minimum:** defined target for first 90 days (bookings or GMV, not vanity clicks).

---

## 2. Supply–demand check

- **Supply:** host pipeline count, time-to-publish, verification capacity.  
- **Demand:** marketing budget, CAC assumption, conversion benchmarks from nearest liquid city.  
- **Rule:** do not overspend guest acquisition until minimum listing count is credible (set per market).

---

## 3. Localization

- **Currency** display and settlement alignment.  
- **Language** (FR/EN in Québec; EN primary elsewhere in Canada).  
- **Regulations** — STR rules, tax collection, insurance wording.  
- **Content** — city LP, FAQs, host onboarding copy.

---

## 4. Onboard hosts

- Agency outreach + direct + referrals (`ref_kind=HOST` where used).  
- Fast verification path; clear fee schedule.  
- Track: drafts → published → first booking.

---

## 5. Launch marketing

- Coordinated: paid, organic, PR if appropriate.  
- UTMs + CRM `source`; weekly creative review ([marketing-machine.md](marketing-machine.md)).

---

## 6. Monitor performance

- **Dashboards:** `/admin/growth-dashboard`, `/admin/enterprise-dashboard`.  
- **Trust:** disputes, refunds, review quality week 1–4.

---

## 7. Iterate

- Cut underperforming ads; double down on supply if conversion is strong but inventory thin.  
- Document learnings in [domination-review.md](domination-review.md) or weekly growth doc.

---

## City rollout checklist (copy for each launch)

- [ ] Legal sign-off for STR + payments  
- [ ] Support macros + staffing window  
- [ ] Trust queue staffed  
- [ ] City LP live + indexed  
- [ ] Host SLA communicated  
- [ ] Success metrics and review date set
