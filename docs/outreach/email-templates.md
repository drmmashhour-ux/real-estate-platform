# Email templates

**Rules:** Under ~120 words each; no hype metrics; no fabricated traction; direct tone.

Replace `[ ]` with real details. Use your real URLs for landing, demo, and deck.

---

## 1. Cold email (short)

**Subject:** B2B SaaS — real estate operations

Hi [Name],

I’m [Your name], building [product name] — a connected platform for real estate brokerages (CRM, offers, contracts, documents, scheduling, and commissions in one place).

We’re [stage / geography] and speaking with investors who care about B2B workflow and vertical SaaS. Would you have **15 minutes** in the next two weeks?

If yes, I’ll send a calendar link — or happy to share our one-pager first.

[Your name]  
[Title] | [Company]  
[Email]

---

## 2. Cold email (slightly longer)

**Subject:** Seed — PropTech / B2B workflow

Hi [Name],

I’m reaching out because [one sentence of real personalization — fund thesis, portfolio company, or focus].

I’m building [product name]: an **operating system for real estate transactions** — one workspace for client pipeline, offers, contracts, shared document rooms, messaging, and financial visibility. Built for brokerages and teams that today juggle too many disconnected tools.

We’re raising [round] and are selective about fit. If this fits your thesis, could we book **15–20 minutes**?

Best,  
[Your name]  
[Link to landing page — optional single link]

## 4. Follow-up #1 (3–5 days)

[One-line factual update if you have one — e.g. “We shipped X” or “We’re focusing on [segment]” — no vanity metrics.]

Thanks,  
[Your name]
