# Follow-up system

## When to follow up

| Stage | Typical window | Goal |
|-------|----------------|------|
| After first email / DM | 3–5 business days | Polite reminder + small value add. |
| After follow-up 1 | 7–10 days from first | Very short “still here.” |
| Final | 14–21 days from first | Close loop; leave door open. |

**Stop** if they reply — even “not now” — and record in your tracker.

---

## How many times

- **Up to 3** follow-ups after the initial message (4 touches total including the first) is a common professional cadence.
- If they open or engage but don’t reply, you may still follow the same cadence; do not assume intent from opens alone.

---

## Tone by follow-up

**Follow-up 1**

- Polite reminder.
- Add **small value**: demo link, one factual product update, or a relevant article — not a wall of text.

**Follow-up 2**

- Very short.
- “Just checking in” is fine if you have nothing new — do not invent news.

**Final**

- Acknowledge you’re closing the thread.
- Thank them for their time.
- Leave door open for future.

---

## Why follow-ups matter

**Most replies** arrive after follow-ups — not after the first message. Consistency is a process, not pressure.

---

## Tools

- Use your **outreach tracker** (see **outreach-tracker-template.md**) for next follow-up dates.
- Set calendar reminders or Notion/CRM reminders — do not rely on memory.
