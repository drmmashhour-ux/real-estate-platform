# Messaging angles

Use these to **test what resonates** — vary one angle per cohort; track replies in your tracker.

---

## Angle 1 — “Operating system for real estate transactions”

**Explanation:** Positions the product as the **single layer** where deals run — not a feature, but the environment.

**When to use:** Investors who understand **platform** or **infrastructure** plays; B2B SaaS generalists.

---

## Angle 2 — “All-in-one platform replacing CRM + contracts + documents + messaging”

**Explanation:** Concrete **replacement** narrative — fewer tools, less fragmentation.

**When to use:** Investors who’ve backed **workflow consolidation** or **vertical SaaS**; when pain is tool sprawl.

---

## Angle 3 — “Workflow automation for brokers and agencies”

**Explanation:** Emphasizes **process** and **operators** — brokerages and teams as users.

**When to use:** Investors who prefer **clear ICP** (brokers, agencies) over abstract “platform” language.

---

## Angle 4 — “Multi-tenant SaaS for real estate teams”

**Explanation:** Signals **B2B**, **multi-tenant**, **team** product — good for SaaS investors.

**When to use:** Technical or SaaS-focused funds; when you want to stress **delivery model** and scalability.

---

## How to iterate

- Pick **one angle** per batch of outreach.
- Track reply rate by angle in **Notes** or a simple tally.
- **Weekly:** double down on the angle that gets meetings, not just opens.

**Do not** claim metrics you have not measured. Describe what the product does and who it’s for.
