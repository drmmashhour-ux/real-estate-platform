# LinkedIn templates

**Rules:** 2–4 lines max per block; conversational; no spam; no fake metrics.

---

## 1. Connection request

**Note (300 chars max on many accounts — shorten if needed):**

Hi [Name] — I’m building B2B workflow software for real estate teams. Would love to connect and share a short note if relevant to your thesis.

— [Your first name]

---

## 2. First message after connection

Thanks for connecting, [Name].

We’re building [product name] — one platform for brokerages to run CRM, deals, contracts, and documents. If you’re looking at PropTech / vertical SaaS, happy to share a **15-min** overview or a link to our site.

---

## 3. Follow-up message

Hi [Name] — quick bump on my last note. If you’d prefer a **demo link** or deck, I can send that instead of a call.

---

## 4. Short pitch message

[Product name] = operating layer for real estate brokerages: pipeline, offers, contracts, documents, messaging, finance — connected. Raising [round]. Open to **15–20 min** if it fits your thesis.
