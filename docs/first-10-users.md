# First 10 users — acquisition scripts (BNHub / LECIPM)

Goal: **10 real users** (mix of **hosts** and **guests**), real usage, and a **repeatable** outreach loop. Pair with **`first-10-tracking.md`** and (optional) **`/admin/early-users`**.

---

## Principles

- **Short messages** — mobile-first; respect platform rules (no spam, no scraping ToS violations).
- **Speed to yes** — offer to set them up in **under 5 minutes** when they bite.
- **Log everything** — every contact in the tracker same day.

---

## Host acquisition — opening

Use when DM, email, or short call:

> Hi! I’m building **BNHub**, a new platform helping property owners get **more bookings** with **lower fees** and **better visibility**.
>
> We’re onboarding a **small group of early hosts** right now and **promoting them heavily**.
>
> Would you like to try it and get **early exposure**?

---

## Host acquisition — follow-up

Send **24–48h** after no reply, max **2** follow-ups:

> Just checking in — we’re **actively promoting** early users and listings right now, so it’s a **good time to join**.
>
> We can **help you set up your listing** and bring you your **first bookings**.

---

## Guest acquisition — opening

> Hey! I’m testing a new platform to find stays with **better prices** and **verified listings**.
>
> We’re offering **early users** exclusive deals and **better support**.
>
> Want to try it and **give feedback**?

---

## Guest acquisition — follow-up

> Just checking in — we’re **actively promoting** early users and listings right now, so it’s a **good time to join**.

(Adapt tone slightly from host version if it feels repetitive in the same channel.)

---

## Closing (hosts + guests)

When they say yes:

> Great — I’ll **get you set up now**. It takes **less than 5 minutes**.

Then: send link, screenshare, or do it for them — **same session**.

---

## Objections (one-liners)

| They say | You say |
|----------|---------|
| “I’m full on Airbnb” | “Totally — this is **extra distribution**, not a replacement. Early hosts get **featured** while we’re small.” |
| “Sounds like work” | “We’ll **import photos** or walk through **one screen** together — 5 min.” |
| “Is it safe?” | “**Verified listings**, secure payments, and we’re **live in [city]** — happy to show the flow.” |

---

## Daily execution targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| **People contacted** | **20** / day |
| **Replies** | **~5** / day (stretch; track actual) |
| **Signups** | **2–3** / day (stretch) |

Stop when you have **10 active** early users you’ve **spoken to** — then switch to **`7day-execution-plan.md`** scale mode.

---

## Repeatable system

1. **Source list** — 50 names (hosts from communities you’re allowed to use; guests from local groups).  
2. **Batch** — same hour: 10 DMs using host **or** guest script (don’t mix tone in one batch).  
3. **Track** — **`first-10-tracking.md`** or **`/admin/early-users`**.  
4. **Next day** — follow-ups first, then new contacts.
