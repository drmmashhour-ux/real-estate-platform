# Investor outreach — BNHub + LECIPM

**Principles:** Short, specific, no inflated traction. **Warm intros** beat cold spray. Follow up **politely** with a single new data point when possible.

---

## Targeting

- **Fit:** Marketplace, proptech, travel-adjacent, **Canada**-friendly funds/angels.  
- **Cheque** size matches round (seed vs pre-seed).  
- **Conflict check:** Portfolio OTAs or direct competitors — still ok if disclosed early.

---

## Email (cold or warm intro)

**Subject:** BNHub / LECIPM — integrated stays + real estate platform ([stage])

---

Hi [Name],

I’m [Founder], building **BNHub** within **LECIPM** — an integrated platform for **short-term rentals and real-estate workflows**, with a strong focus on **trust, transparency, and operational automation** (including AI where we’ve shipped it).

We’re **[stage: e.g. raising seed / in pilot / pre-revenue with early users]** and preparing for our next phase. I’m not asking for a commitment — just whether you’d be open to a **short intro call** to see if there’s fit.

Happy to share a deck and live product walkthrough.

Best,  
[Name]  
[Title] · [Company]  
[Email] · [Link: calendar or LinkedIn]

---

## Follow-up process

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| T+0 | Send intro email or thank-you for warm intro |
| T+5–7 | One follow-up: new fact (“shipped X”, “N pilot hosts”) or question |
| T+14 | Final light bump — “closing process timing” only if true |
| After “no” | Thank; ask permission to update quarterly **one-pager** |

**Don’t:** Mass BCC, fake FOMO, or “we’re oversubscribed” unless legally true.

---

## Materials to attach / link

- **Deck** PDF or Docs link (view-only).  
- **One-pager** — problem, solution, stage, ask, contact.  
- **Data room** — after serious interest (cap table, contracts, metrics export).

---

## Example (user-requested style)

> Hi, I’m building BNHub, an integrated real-estate and short-term rental platform — with AI-assisted operations where we’ve shipped them, and a clear focus on trust and transparency.  
> We’re currently growing and preparing for our next phase.  
> Would you be open to a short introduction call?

*(Prefer the longer template above for institutional investors; this works for soft intros.)*
