# Domination weekly review — growth, revenue, expansion

**Cadence:** weekly, 60–90 minutes. **Participants:** growth, supply, finance (optional), product (optional).

**Inputs:** `/admin/growth-dashboard`, ad platforms, support top issues, trust metrics.

---

## 1. Growth

- **Users / hosts:** vs prior week and vs target ramp.  
- **Domination cities:** listings, bookings, GMV per city row — who wins, who lags?  
- **Network effect:** bookings per 1k listings — improving or flat?  
- **Funnel:** traffic → signup → search → checkout (best available proxies).

**Decisions:** budget shifts, city pause/play, creative kills.

---

## 2. Revenue

- **GMV** and **net take** (commission + featured + subs).  
- **CAC** and **payback** by channel.  
- **Concentration risk:** one city or one host too large?

**Decisions:** pricing experiments, featured inventory packages, host premium promos.

---

## 3. Expansion

- **Next city gate:** liquidity thresholds met? Ops + support ready?  
- **Agency pipeline:** signed partners, listings in pipeline.  
- **Regulatory / trust:** blockers for Toronto/Vancouver or new provinces?

**Decisions:** go / no-go for next geo wave; hire or contract needs.

---

## 4. Risks & incidents

- Fraud spikes, chargebacks, viral negative reviews.  
- Infra: latency, error rates on search/checkout.
