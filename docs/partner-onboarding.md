# Partner onboarding — enterprise / B2B (BNHub)

After **closed won**, run this checklist. Adjust for legal and product maturity.

---

## 1. Kickoff (Day 0–2)

- Introduce **account owner** (sales) and **onboarding** (ops).  
- Confirm **pilot scope**: listing count, cities, timeline.  
- Share [partnerships.md](partnerships.md) integration expectations if API/tools involved.

---

## 2. Collect listing data

- Addresses, photos, amenities, house rules, pricing baseline.  
- Preferred **calendar** source (native, iCal, channel manager).  
- Tax / regulatory disclosures required in their jurisdiction.

---

## 3. Verify listings

- Identity and **listing verification** per trust policy ([trust-compliance.md](trust-compliance.md)).  
- Fix blockers before publish; document exceptions with approver.

---

## 4. Publish listings

- Move to **PUBLISHED** (or equivalent) when criteria met.  
- QA search visibility and booking test (internal or partner-led).

---

## 5. Monitor performance

- **Weekly during pilot:** bookings, GMV, cancellation rate, support tickets.  
- Compare to baseline they provided (if any) — no implied guarantee if baseline missing.  
- **Decision:** expand, adjust, or end pilot with lessons learned.

---

## 6. Expand or contract

- **Expand:** more listings, optional premium features, marketing package.  
- **Contract:** pause new onboarding; keep reporting clear and professional.

---

## Artifacts to store

- Signed terms (where applicable)  
- Onboarding checklist completion date  
- CRM link (`enterprise_leads` id) and any internal partner id
