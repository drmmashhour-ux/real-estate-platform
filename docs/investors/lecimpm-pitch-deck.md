## Slide 2 — Problem

- **Most real estate platforms are passive** — listings and traffic, limited execution help  
- **Brokers lose high-quality leads** — slow routing, weak prioritization, leaky follow-up  
- **Nothing systematically improves deal success** — milestones and negotiation vary by broker habit  
- **No unified intelligence layer** across listings, deals, leads, and compliance signals  

---

## Slide 3 — Market gap

**Today’s categories**

- **Centris** → listing distribution & syndication (reach, not execution depth)  
- **CRMs** → storage & tasks (data in, limited optimization out)  

**What’s missing**

- **Intelligence** across funnel, deal file, and marketplace  
- **Optimization** — ranking, pricing posture, cohort quality (human-approved where needed)  
- **Automation that assists** — not autonomous messaging or substitute for brokerage duties  

---

## Slide 4 — Solution

**LECIPM**

- **Captures & attributes leads** — sources, campaigns, partner channels where configured  
- **Analyzes deals** — scores, probabilities, risk bands (**advisory**, not legal status)  
- **Guides brokers** — AI assistant with **next-step suggestions only** (no auto-send to clients)  
- **Surfaces risks** — compliance & insurance-aligned signals (**assistive**, not a law firm)  
- **Learns from outcomes** — recorded patterns improve defaults over time (bounded, review-friendly)  

---

## Slide 5 — Product (system overview)

**Core modules**

- **Lead Funnel Engine** — ingestion, scoring, attribution, CRM pipeline  
- **Deal Intelligence Engine** — `dealScore`, close probability, suggested actions  
- **AI Broker Assistant** — reminders, alerts, actions (**broker chooses when to act**)  
- **Compliance + Insurance Layer** — checklists, snapshots, audit-friendly trails  
- **Investment & Learning engines** — ranking / ROI **bands** + outcome patterns → smarter scoring (**signals, not advice**)  

---

## Slide 6 — How it works

**Flow**

1. **Listing** — partner feeds (e.g. Centris) **plus** native LECIPM surfaces  
2. **Lead captured** — enriched with behavior and attribution  
3. **Deal tracked** — milestones, intelligence refresh on activity  
4. **AI suggests actions** — broker-reviewed; **no unauthorized client outreach**  
5. **System learns** — outcomes and patterns feed back into models  
6. **Better outcomes** — faster follow-up, clearer pipeline, safer operations (**measured, not guaranteed**)

👉 **LECIPM is an AI operating system for real estate** — humans stay accountable; software assists, ranks, and learns within explicit rails
