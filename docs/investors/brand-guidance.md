# LECIPM Manager — brand guidance (investor materials)

## Colors

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|--------|
| **Gold** | `#D4AF37` | Accents, key numbers, icons, rules, CTAs |
| **Black** | `#0b0b0b` | Primary background |
| **Supporting** | `#1a1a1a` | Cards, panels |
| **Text primary** | `#ffffff` | Headlines |
| **Text secondary** | `#bfbfbf` | Body, captions |

## Style

- **Minimal** — generous whitespace; no decorative clutter.
- **Luxury** — restrained gold; **no** rainbow gradients or stock “startup” tropes.
- **Clean spacing** — align to a simple grid; consistent vertical rhythm.

## Rules

- **One idea per slide** whenever possible.
- **Maximum 5 bullets** per slide in decks and one-pagers.
- **No clutter** — one focal stat beats six small numbers.

## Fonts

- **Modern sans-serif** (e.g. Inter, Manrope, or equivalent).
- **Headings:** bold, clear hierarchy.
- **Body:** readable at **16px+** equivalent in slide tools.

## Export

- **PDF one-pager:** single page, embedded fonts or outlined text.
- **Deck:** 16:9; test **contrast** on projector.
