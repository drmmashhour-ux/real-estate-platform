## Revenue scenarios (illustrative)

## Unit economics (illustrative)

**Revenue per booking (blended, formula):**

`Fee per booking + (lead conversion × lead value) + (upsell attach × upsell $)`

Example (base scenario, spread across all bookings):  
$20 + (~$2.16) + (~$2.00) ≈ **~$24** blended **illustrative** platform revenue per booking before variable costs.

**High-margin layers:** **Insurance leads** and **upsells** are typically **higher margin** than the core fee after payment processing and partner splits — exact **net** depends on contracts (not modeled here in detail).

**Note:** This is **not a ramp model** — it does **not** show Month 1→24 growth. Build a **time-phased** spreadsheet for fundraising diligence.
