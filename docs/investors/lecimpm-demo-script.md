# LECIPM — Live demo script

**Duration target:** ~12–18 minutes + Q&A  
**Tone:** Confident, precise — **never claim guaranteed ROI or autonomous legal compliance**  
**Prep:** Know exact URLs for staging/prod · log in as demo broker/admin before the call  

---

## Intro (~30 sec)

“Today, many real estate platforms **stop at listings**. They **don’t systematically help teams close**.

**LECIPM** is different: we turn interactions into **structured intelligence** — leads, deals, compliance signals, and learning — so brokers can **move faster with less leakage**.

Let me show you how.”

---

## Demo step 1 — Listing (~2 min)

**Show:** A **property / listing detail** page (CRM or public browse — whichever is deployed for your audience).

**Say:**

“This property may appear on **syndicated channels** and on **LECIPM**. Syndication brings reach; **LECIPM adds the operational layer** — scoring, funnel context, and downstream deal intelligence **when the workflow is connected**.”

**Optional click:** Point out **LEC-specific** panels if visible (trust/summary cards, conversion CTAs).

---

## Demo step 2 — Lead capture (~2 min)

**Show:** **Lead capture** — contact form, inquiry, or Immo/widget path **as implemented** in your environment.

**Say:**

“When someone engages, we **capture the lead** with **source and campaign attribution** — not just a spreadsheet row. That powers routing, scoring, and reporting.”

**Fine print (if asked):** “Consent and Law 25-aligned flows are **configured per surface** — we don’t bypass privacy rules for speed.”

---

## Demo step 3 — Deal engine (~3 min)

**Show:** **Deal detail** with **Deal Intelligence** / score panel (`dealScore`, risk, suggested next step — routes like `/dashboard/deals/[id]` if available).

**Say:**

“Every tracked deal gets an **intelligence snapshot**: **deal score**, **close probability**, **risk band**. These are **decision support signals** — they **don’t replace** legal status or the brokerage file.”

---

## Demo step 4 — AI assistant (~2 min)

**Show:** **Broker assistant** floating panel or dashboard — **suggestions list** (ACTION / ALERT / REMINDER).

**Say:**

“The assistant tells the broker **what to consider next** — follow-ups, stale pipeline, deal milestones. **Critically: we do not auto-send messages to clients.** The broker **decides** every outbound touch.”

---

## Demo step 5 — Compliance (~2 min)

**Show:** **Compliance or insurance visibility** — checklist, snapshot, or dashboard strip (co-ownership / insurance where modeled).

**Say:**

“We **surface risk early** — disclosure gaps, checklist status, insurance posture **where data exists**. This **reduces operational surprises**; it’s **assistive**, not a substitute for mandated brokerage obligations.”

---

## Demo step 6 — Investment engine (~2 min)

**Show:** **Investment / opportunity** surface — CRM listing ranking, BNHub investor stance, or **`/dashboard/investor`** portfolio tools **as relevant to your demo tenant**.

**Say:**

“For qualifying inventory we produce **ranking and ROI-style bands** from **real platform data** — enough to **prioritize diligence**, not to replace an investor’s own analysis or suitability review.”

---

## Demo step 7 — Learning loop (~1 min)

**Show:** Optional **admin traction** or **learning patterns** screen if migrated — otherwise explain verbally.

**Say:**

“Terminal deal outcomes feed **pattern summaries** that **nudge scoring over time**. The system **gets sharper with usage**, within **bounded, auditable** changes — not black-box autonomy.”

---

## Closing (~30 sec)

“**LECIPM isn’t another listing site.** It’s an **AI operating layer** on top of real transactions — **lead → deal → compliance → learning**.

We’re building **measurable improvement** in conversion and execution — **with humans in charge** — and that’s how we **earn the right** to scale.”

---

## Fallback lines (if something breaks live)

- “This surface is behind a **feature flag** in this environment — here’s the **screenshot / recording**.”  
- “Numbers on traction slides are **internal placeholders** until we finalize the audit period.”
