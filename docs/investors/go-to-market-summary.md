# LECIPM Manager — go-to-market summary (one page)

## Wedge

Start with **short-term rental (STR) hosts** and **bookable inventory** — prove **density** and **repeat behavior** in a **defined geography** (e.g. **Canada / Quebec**) before broad **North American** spend.

## First ~100 users (guests)

- **Measured** discovery: **SEO** and **intent** content tied to **real** listings.
- **Short-form** and **local** storytelling — **quality** over viral noise.
- **Trust-first** UX: **transparent** fees, clear **booking status**, **notifications**.
- **Referral** prompts **after** a successful stay — **tracked**, not vanity.

## Growth loops

- **Content + marketplace:** every **strong** page links to **bookable** supply.
- **Host success → more listings → better discovery → more bookings** — classic **marketplace flywheel**, executed **locally** first.
- **AI marketing** assists **structure and iteration**; **humans** approve **claims** and **compliance**.

## Retention (AI value)

- **AI pricing and promotion suggestions** — hosts **see yield**; product becomes **habitual**.
- **Messaging and alerts** — fewer dropped threads, faster **resolution**.
- **Upsells and opt-in leads** — **incremental** revenue **without** opaque guest charges.

## Expansion path

- **STR** core proven → adjacent **real estate** and **operator** workflows on the **same** stack.
- **Lead monetization** (insurance / partners) as a **revenue multiplier** alongside **booking fees**.
- **Geographic** expansion only with **repeat cohorts** and **acceptable** CAC / contribution **per booking**.

**Note:** All **numeric** targets belong in your **internal** plan — keep investor-facing GTM **strategic** unless sharing **verified** metrics.
