## Company overview

**LECIPM** is a **luxury, multi-hub real estate platform** that unifies **residential marketplace workflows** and **BNHub short-term stays** on one architecture. It is designed as an **operating system**: **role-aware hubs**, **centralized admin control**, **revenue-aware analytics**, and **multi-channel notifications** across **web (Next.js)** and **mobile (Expo)**.

---

## Problem

Real estate operators still depend on **disconnected tools**—CRM, spreadsheets, separate STR stack—so **money, risk, and service quality** are hard to reconcile. Marketplaces optimize **discovery**, not **continuous operational control** for brokers, sellers, investors, hosts, and leadership.

---

## Solution

**One platform, six hubs:** Buyer, Seller, Broker, Investor, BNHub (stays), and Admin—each with dedicated surfaces; **admin** retains cross-hub visibility.  
**AI** supports **insights, anomaly awareness, and analysis** within **governance-oriented** workflows (not unaccountable automation).  
**Notifications** tie to **business events** (bookings, revenue moments, operational signals) via **push, email, and SMS** routing—not generic marketing noise.

## Product

| Layer | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Discovery** | Homepage, listings search & detail; BNHub search & stay detail. |
| **Hubs** | Buyer / seller / broker / investor dashboards grounded in **service-layer** data. |
