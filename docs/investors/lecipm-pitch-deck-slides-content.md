## Slide 2 — Problem

**Title:** The platform stack stopped at listings  

**Bullets:**

- Passive directories → **brokers bleed leads** after the click  
- **No unified intelligence** across CRM, deals, and marketplace  
- Deals succeed or fail on **individual habit**, not system leverage  
- Regulators expect **auditability** — spreadsheets don’t scale  

---

## Slide 3 — Market gap

**Title:** Distribution ≠ execution  

**Bullets:**

- Syndication (**e.g. Centris**) → **reach**  
- Legacy CRM → **storage**, limited optimization  
- **Missing:** intelligence, conversion discipline, workflow depth  

---

## Slide 4 — Solution

**Title:** One operating layer for transactions  

**Bullets:**

- **Captures & attributes leads** end-to-end  
- **Analyzes deals** — scores, risk, suggested next steps (**assistive**)  
- **Guides brokers** — assistants that **suggest**, not auto-send  
- **Learns from outcomes** — tighter defaults over time
