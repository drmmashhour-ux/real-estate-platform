## 30 seconds

“LECIPM is an **AI-managed real estate operating system**. We unify **buyers, sellers, brokers, investors, and BNHub stays** on **one luxury platform**—with **governance**, **revenue-aware control**, and **real-time notifications** across **web and mobile**. Operators get a **command center**, not another disconnected marketplace.”

## 90 seconds

“Real estate still runs across **fragmented tools**: CRM here, spreadsheets there, stays on a separate app. Money and risk surface **too late**.

LECIPM is built as a **multi-hub marketplace OS**: **Buyer, Seller, Broker, Investor, BNHub, and Admin** share one stack. **Payments and platform events** roll into **exec-ready dashboards**. **AI** delivers **insights and anomaly awareness** inside **governance guardrails**—we’re not pretending software replaces regulation or judgment.

We ship **web and mobile** with **authenticated, lightweight APIs** so leadership isn’t desk-tethered. **Notifications** route over **push, email, and SMS** on **business events**—bookings, revenue moments, operational alerts.

We’re **Canada-first**, **luxury-positioned**, and focused on **operational credibility** over vanity metrics.”

## 3 minutes (investor narrative)

**Hook**  
“I’m building LECIPM—an **AI-managed real estate operating system**. Not a niche listing site: a **single platform** where luxury residential workflows and short-term stays can share **identity, payments, analytics, and alerts**.”

**Problem**  
“Teams still stitch together **five tools** to run brokerage + hospitality + investor reporting. **Risk is reactive.** Revenue is hard to reconcile with operations.”

**Solution**  
“LECIPM is intentionally a **multi-hub architecture**: each persona gets a real dashboard, but **admin** sees cross-hub **movements and revenue**. We implement **platform payments** patterns and **event-driven notifications** so money and exceptions are visible **as they happen**.”

**Differentiation**  
“Our differentiation is **control**: **governance-aware** workflows, **revenue-aware** dashboards, and **multi-channel notifications**—plus **mobile** so operators aren’t chained to a desktop.”

**AI**  
“AI is a **layer**: insights, detection, admin analysis—**roadmapped** autonomy stays inside **policy and auditability**, not autonomous chaos.”

**Market**  
“We’re credible **Canada / Quebec-first**, bilingual-ready, **premium** positioning—expand once unit economics and compliance paths are proven.”

**Traction framing**  
“We align investors on **readiness**: broad surfaces shipped, settlement and notification plumbing in motion—**we don’t substitute fabricated KPIs** for credibility.”

**Ask**  
“We’re raising for **engineering scale**, **pilots**, and **partnerships**—to turn this architecture into dominant share in our wedge.”
