# LECIPM Manager — investor FAQ

**Rule:** Do not invent numbers. Use **illustrative** only when referring to [financial-projections.md](./financial-projections.md).

---

### Why not just use Airbnb?

Airbnb and similar OTAs own **global habit** and **supply breadth**. We are not claiming to **replace** them on day one. **LECIPM Manager** targets **operators** who want **AI-assisted pricing and promos**, **integrated** booking and **trust workflows**, and **transparent** economics — starting with a **focused geography** and **STR host** wedge.

---

### What is the AI advantage — really?

AI **reduces manual work per booking**: **pricing**, **promotional timing**, **merchandising** for conversion, and **communication assistance**. That is **operating leverage** — not a wrapper on a chatbot. **Trust, policy, and disputes** remain **human-governed**.

---

### What is the GTM strategy?

**Supply-first wedge:** onboard **quality STR hosts** and **listings**; prove **repeat bookings** in a **defined market** (e.g. Canada / Quebec). **Demand** via **content**, **SEO**, **referrals**, and **small measured** paid tests. **Retention** through **AI pricing value** and **notifications**.

---

### How do you make money?

**Booking fees** on completed stays (**illustrative fee range 10–20%** of AOV in models — actual terms apply). **Qualified insurance / partner leads** (opt-in, disclosed). **Checkout upsells**. Optional future **premium host tools** if **ROI** is proven.

---

### What are the main risks?

**Execution risk** achieving **liquidity** per city. **Regulatory** STR and **lead-gen** compliance. **Competitive** pressure from **large OTAs**. **Technology** — payments, fraud, AI quality — mitigated by **staged rollout** and **human oversight** where required.

---

### What is defensibility?

Not a single feature — **supply relationships**, **operational data** (what price and promo **converts** in **your** markets), and **host habit** on **your** stack. **AI** improves with that **loop**; it is **not** magic moat on day one.

---

### Why now?

**AI** is usable in **production workflows**. **Travel** patterns favor **flexible** stays. **Hosts** are **professionalizing** and will adopt tools that **save time** and **lift yield**.

---

### What is your current stage?

**Platform built. Soft launch ready.** **End-to-end** booking and **payment** paths and **host-side** capabilities in the product. **Replace with verified KPIs** when you have them — **do not** fabricate traction in meetings.
