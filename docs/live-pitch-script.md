# Live investor pitch script — BNHub + LECIPM

**Use:** In-person or video calls. **Replace** bracketed placeholders with your real facts. **Do not** invent metrics — if traction is thin, keep the validation lines below as written.

**Length:** About **5–7 minutes** when spoken at a calm pace (with pauses). **Time yourself** — if you’re under five minutes, add **one** real customer or pilot story in **Traction**; if you’re over seven, trim **Market** or **Growth** first.  
**Also:** [60-second](#60-second-version) and [2-minute](#2-minute-version) versions at the end.

**Delivery:** Quick tips below; full guide in **[pitch-delivery.md](pitch-delivery.md)**.

## Delivery tips (in this file)

**How to speak:** One thought per breath. Sound like **you** — not a press release. Glance at slides, then **eyes back** to the room or camera. Land the bold phrases with a **small** lift in tone, not a shout.

**Pacing:** Aim **~130–150 words per minute**. Slow down on **traction** and the **ask**. If you run long, trim **market** or **growth** first — **never** cut the ask or honesty on stage.

**Confidence:** Know **one** traction fact and **one** 90-day milestone **cold**. If tempted by hype words, swap for **specifics** — *pilot*, *listings*, *repeat user*. After “We’re raising…”, **stop** for two seconds.

**Markers:** `[pause]` = let the line **land** (often ~1 second). `[emphasize]` = stress the **phrase before** it (or the **bold** nearby).

**Full playbook:** **[pitch-delivery.md](pitch-delivery.md)** — remote vs in-room, practice schedule, follow-up.
