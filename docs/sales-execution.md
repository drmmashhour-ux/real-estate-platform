# Sales & operations execution

Pipeline, onboarding, and partnerships for **hosts, brokers, and B2B** motion—pairs with **`roles.md`** (Ops/sales).

---

## Principles

- **Speed to first value:** Time-to-first-listing or first booking is a product metric; ops owns the human side.  
- **CRM is source of truth:** If it’s not logged, it didn’t happen.  
- **Feedback loop:** Every objection gets a **tag** (pricing, trust, feature gap) → shared weekly with product.

---

## Daily

| Activity | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Pipeline** | New leads contacted; next steps dated. |
| **Onboarding hosts** | Checklist: account → listing draft → verification → go-live (customize per market). |
| **Support** | SLA you define (e.g. 24h first response); escalate bugs to engineering with repro. |
| **Partners** | One follow-up on active partnership threads. |

---

## Weekly

- **Pipeline review:** Stage counts, stuck deals, win/loss reasons.  
- **Onboarding funnel:** Drop-off step; one improvement (template, call, doc).  
- **Revenue:** Forecast vs actual; adjust focus cities or segments.

---

## Closing deals

- **Discovery:** Goals, timeline, decision process.  
- **Demo:** Staging or prod; stick to **`release-strategy.md`** stable flows.  
- **Proposal:** Clear fees, obligations, and support—legal review when material.  
- **Close:** Written confirmation; kick off onboarding checklist.

---

## Partnerships

- **Criteria:** Audience fit, brand risk, technical integration cost.  
- **Process:** Intro → alignment doc → pilot → scale.  
- **Owner:** Founder approves non-standard economics.

---

## Metrics (see `performance.md`)

- Meetings, conversion stage→stage, activated listings, revenue or signed GMV.  
- Host NPS or qualitative check-ins monthly.
