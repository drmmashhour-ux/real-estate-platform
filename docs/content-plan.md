# Content plan — daily engine (first 100 users)

Support **`first-100-users.md`**. Post **2–3 short videos per day** when in sprint mode; **1/day** minimum maintenance.

---

## Themes (rotate)

| # | Angle | Hook line (example) |
|---|--------|---------------------|
| **1** | Finding stays | “Here’s how to spot a **verified** stay in [city] under $X.” |
| **2** | Earning as host | “**Extra bookings** without only living on one app.” |
| **3** | Platform benefits | “**Lower fees**, **local support** — we’re launching BNHub here.” |

---

## Format

- **15–45s** vertical video.  
- **One idea** per video.  
- **CTA:** “Link in bio” → **`/early-access`** or app install.  
- **UTM:** `utm_source=tiktok&utm_campaign=content_daily`

---

## Posting

| Platform | When |
|----------|------|
| **TikTok** | Daily (best slot you can sustain) |
| **Instagram** | Reels — same or next day |
| **Facebook** | Reels or native video in **local** groups (follow group rules) |

---

## Weekly batch (efficient)

- **Monday:** Film **5** clips in one session.  
- **Tue–Fri:** Schedule/drip **1–2**/day.  
- **Sat:** Reply to comments + DM warm leads.

---

## Don’t

- Long intros; start on the **problem** in **2s**.  
- Fake scarcity; use **real** launch facts.  
- Post without checking **comments** for 24h.
