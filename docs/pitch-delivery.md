# Pitch delivery — BNHub + LECIPM

**Companion:** Practice from **[live-pitch-script.md](live-pitch-script.md)**.

---

## How to speak

- **Talk like a person, not a deck.** The slides support you; your **voice** carries the story. Glance at the screen, then **back to the camera or the room**.
- **Short units.** One thought per breath. If a sentence has two commas and a semicolon, **split it**.
- **Land key words** — *integrated*, *trust*, *depth*, *honest milestones* — with a **slight** lift in energy, not a shout.
- **Own the silence.** [pause] in the script is a **feature**: it lets investors **absorb** the problem and the ask.

---

## Pacing

- **Target:** Roughly **130–150 words per minute** for clarity; **slower** on numbers and the ask.
- **5–7 minute body:** If you run long, cut **market** color first, then tighten **growth** — never cut the **ask** or **traction honesty**.
- **Rhythm:** Problem section can feel **heavier** (slightly slower). Solution and product can move **one notch faster** — still calm.
- **Timer:** Rehearse with a stopwatch **three times**; note where you drift +15s and **edit** the script, don’t just talk faster.

---

## Confidence (without hype)

- **Preparation = confidence.** Know your **one** proof point for traction and your **one** milestone for the next 90 days cold.
- **Deflect puffery.** If you feel tempted to say “revolutionary” or “massive,” swap for **specific**: *integrated*, *repeat users*, *pilot with [type of partner]*.
- **Body:** Feet planted (or sit tall), hands visible, **slow** inhale before the hook.
- **Q&A frame:** “Great question — here’s what we know today … here’s what we’re still validating.” **Honesty** reads as strength in early stage.

---

## Remote vs in person

- **Video:** Look at the **lens** for hook and ask — that’s your “eye contact.”
- **Room:** Pick **two** friendly faces if it’s a group; rotate every 30–45 seconds.
- **Audio:** Test **once** before the call; poor audio kills **credibility** faster than a weak slide.

---

## After the pitch

- **Stop talking** after the ask for **two seconds** — let them respond.
- **One-page follow-up** within 24h: thank-you, **one** bullet on fit, link to deck + data they requested.

---

## Practice plan (minimal but effective)

| Session | What to do |
|---------|------------|
| 1 | Read full script **out loud**, mark sticky sentences. |
| 2 | Deliver **without** slides — story only. |
| 3 | Full run with **timer** + **record**; watch **once** for filler words (“um,” “like”). |
| 4 | **60s** and **2m** versions until **smooth** without notes. |

---

**Remember:** Investors fund **clarity** and **execution** under uncertainty — not the loudest pitch. **Calm conviction** wins.
