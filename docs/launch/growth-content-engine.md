# LECIPM — Growth Content Engine

**North star:** Authority + trust for **premium** audiences. **Cadence defaults** assume **one small team**—scale frequency only when replies and pipeline justify it.

---

## Content pillars → topic bank

| Pillar | Topic ideas (rotate) | Primary formats |
|--------|-------------------------|-----------------|
| **Luxury real estate** | Neighborhood spotlights; “what €X buys in [city]”; architecture/staging mistakes; negotiation etiquette | Carousel, Short, voice-over tour |
| **AI in real estate** | How AI **assists** ops (not replaces agents); anomaly awareness; admin command center narrative | Talking head, diagram Short, LinkedIn article |
| **Fraud / risk awareness** | Wire fraud red flags; credential phishing; **verify before deposit** scripts | Story format, checklist carousel, email deep-dive |
| **Pricing & investment** | Cap rates vs emotion; STR vs long-term **trade-offs** (informational); fee transparency | Chart Short, newsletter section |
| **Host / broker growth** | Reply speed benchmarks; listing photo standards; calendar hygiene | Before/after, tutorial Reel |
| **Platform authority** | “How LECIPM hubs connect”; **multi-hub OS** explainer; behind-the-scenes product | Founder clip, UI screen recording |

**Every asset ends with **one CTA**** (pick one): **Save checklist** · **Book demo** · **Join waitlist** · **Start listing wizard** · **Follow for Part 2**.

## Weekly rhythm (minimum viable)

| Channel | Cadence | Role |
|---------|---------|------|
| **TikTok** | **3× / week** | Reach + curiosity; **≤45s** hooks |
| **Instagram** | **3× / week** (2 Reels + 1 carousel **or** 3 Reels) | Aesthetic + trust |
| **LinkedIn** | **2× / week** | Broker / investor / operator credibility |
| **YouTube Shorts** | **2× / week** (repurpose top TikTok/Reels) | Search + longevity |
| **Email** | **1× / week** newsletter + **1× / week** lifecycle (segmented) | Conversion + retention |

**Batch day:** **Monday** script + film **4 Shorts**; **Tuesday** edit; **Wed–Fri** stagger posts + email.

## Measurement (tie to KPI spec)

Track per channel: **saves**, **link clicks**, **demo requests attributed**, **signup UTM**. Weekly: **keep top 2 hooks**, kill bottom 2 formats.

---

## Brand guardrails

- **Black / gold** calm confidence—no shouting; no meme culture unless **on-brand** wit.
- **No unverified market stats**—say “many operators” not “97%” without citation.
- **Disclaimers** where regulated topics appear (not legal/financial advice).
