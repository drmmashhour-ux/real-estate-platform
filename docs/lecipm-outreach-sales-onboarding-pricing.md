## 1. Cold DM / LinkedIn (copy-paste)

**Goal:** Replies → demo → close.

### Message 1 (best — simple)

> Hi [Name],  
> I built a tool that analyzes real estate listings and shows **why buyers hesitate** (missing trust, weak info, etc.).  
> I tested it on a few listings and the results were surprising.  
> Would you be open to me **analyzing one of yours for free**?

### Message 2 (more direct)

> Hi [Name],  
> Quick question — have you ever wondered why **some listings get ignored** even when they look good?  
> I built a system that detects exactly that.  
> I can **run one of your listings through it** if you want.

### Follow-up (if no reply)

> Just wanted to follow up — I can send you a **quick breakdown of one of your listings** (takes 2 minutes).  
> No cost, just curious to get your feedback.

## 2. First 10 sales script (word-for-word)

**Open**

> “Thanks for your time — I’ll show you something quick that might **directly impact your listings**.”

**Show**

> “Let’s take **one of your listings**.”

→ Run analysis (product demo)

**Pain**

> “Here — this is where **buyers usually hesitate**.”

**Value**

> “If we fix these, your listing becomes **much stronger immediately**.”

**Close**

> “Do you want to try this on **your next listing**?”

**Pricing line**

> “We usually charge monthly, but I can give you a **first month trial**.”

## 3. Onboarding UI (step-by-step screens)

**Goal:** User sees value in **&lt; 30 seconds**.

| Screen | Title / state | Primary action |
|--------|----------------|----------------|
| **1 — Entry** | “Check your listing score” | **Start** |
| **2 — Input** | Paste listing URL **or** create listing | Continue |
| **3 — Loading** | “Analyzing your listing…” | — |
| **4 — Result** | **Trust Score** · **Issues** · **Deal Score** | Next |
| **5 — Hook** | “Fix these to improve your listing” *(avoid fake +% unless backed by data)* | Continue |
| **6 — Action** | **Fix issues** | Links to seller hub / checklist |
| **7 — Upgrade** | **Unlock verified badge** | Pricing / verification flow |

*Implementation note:* Wire screens to **deterministic** TrustGraph + Deal Analyzer outputs; no invented scores.
