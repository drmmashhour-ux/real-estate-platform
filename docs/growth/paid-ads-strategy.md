# LECIPM — Paid ads strategy

A paid acquisition system to bring **brokers** and **buyers** to LECIPM: where to run, who to target, how to structure campaigns, and how to measure what works.

---

## Platforms

| Platform | Surfaces | Role |
|----------|----------|------|
| **1. Meta** | Facebook, Instagram (Feed, Reels, Stories) | Broker demos, buyer interest, creative testing |
| **2. Google Ads** | Search, Display | High-intent search + remarketing |

---

## Target audience

### Brokers

- Real estate agents in **Québec**  
- **Interests / affinities:** real estate, **MLS**, **Centris**, broker tools, lead generation, property marketing  

### Buyers

- People **searching for properties** (rent, buy, invest)  
- **Real estate investors** and value-driven buyers who respond to analysis and listings  

Tighten segments in each ad set (geography, language, lookalikes from converters) as you collect data.

---

## Campaign structure

### Meta — Campaign 1: Broker acquisition

| Field | Value |
|--------|--------|
| **Goal** | Leads or traffic to a **Book demo** flow |
| **Creative angle** | *“Get more leads and close faster with AI”* |
| **CTA** | **Book demo** (e.g. to contact or Calendly; match LECIPM landing) |
| **Landing** | Broker-focused LECIPM page (e.g. `FEATURE_LANDING_V1` home, `/contact`, or dedicated broker LP) |

### Meta — Campaign 2: Buyer leads

| Field | Value |
|--------|--------|
| **Goal** | Leads, traffic, or engagement to listing / analysis |
| **Creative angle** | *“See full property analysis with AI”* |
| **CTA** | **View property** (or *Get analysis* if you lead to evaluate or a listing) |
| **Landing** | Listings browse, `/evaluate`, or listing detail — align copy with destination |

Rotate 2–3 creatives per campaign; kill losers after ~1–3k impressions per variant.

---

### Google Ads — Search

Start with phrase / exact keywords (adjust match types as conversion data grows):

| Theme | Example keywords |
|--------|------------------|
| Buyer intent | `buy house Montreal`, `homes for sale Montreal`, `Montreal real estate listing` |
| Broker / agent | `real estate broker Quebec`, `Montreal real estate agent` |
| Investor | `investment property Quebec`, `investment property Canada` |

Add **negative keywords** early (jobs, salary, courses, Zillow clones you don’t compete with, etc.). Point search ads to URLs that mirror the keyword intent (listing search vs broker page).

---

### Google Ads — Display

- **Retarget** site visitors who didn’t convert  
- Optionally **dynamic** display showing **listings** if you feed a catalog  
- Exclude low-quality placements over time

## Ad copy (starter)

**RSA / Meta primary text**

| Role | Copy |
|------|------|
| **Headline** | AI real estate platform |
| **Description** | Optimize listings, capture leads, and close faster with LECIPM. |

Use Québec French variants for FR campaigns; keep claims compliant with brokerage / advertising rules in your jurisdiction.

---

## Budget

| Phase | Guidance |
|-------|----------|
| **Start** | **US$10–20/day per campaign** (or CAD equivalent — pick one currency per account for clarity) |
| **Scale** | Increase budget on audiences/creatives with acceptable **cost per qualified lead** and downstream conversion |
| **Protect** | Cap learning-phase overspend; avoid big jumps on Meta (≤20–30% per few days where possible) |

---

## Funnel

```
Ad → Landing page → Lead capture → Follow-up → Conversion
```

- Ads must match landing promise (broker vs buyer).  
- Lead capture: email/phone + clear next step (demo booked, analysis sent, listing saved).  

---

## Tracking

| Layer | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Conversion pixel** | Meta Pixel + Google Ads conversion actions (thank-you page, demo booked, signup) |
| **UTM tracking** | `utm_source`, `utm_medium`, `utm_campaign`, `utm_content` on every destination URL |
| **Lead source** | Persist UTM or campaign ID in CRM / DB so sales and product see origin |

---

## Metrics

Track weekly at minimum:

| Metric | Why |
|--------|-----|
| **Cost per lead (CPL)** | Efficiency of spend |
| **Conversion rate** | Landing + form quality |
| **ROI / ROAS** | Revenue or pipeline vs ad spend (define “conversion value” for your stage) |

Segment by platform, campaign, creative, and geography.
