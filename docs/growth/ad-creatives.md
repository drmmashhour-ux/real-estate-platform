# LECIPM — Ad creatives (Meta + Google)

Ready-to-brief design and copy for paid social and display. Align visual files with brand: **black background**, **gold (#D4AF37) accents**, minimal type, fast read at small sizes.

---

## Formats

| Ratio | Use |
|-------|-----|
| **Square 1:1** | Meta Feed, Google Display square units |
| **Vertical 9:16** | Instagram Reels, Stories, Meta Reels placements |

Export **safe zones**: keep headline and CTA inside central 4:5 “safe” crop for cross-placements.

---

## Ad set 1 — Broker acquisition

**Goal:** Book demos / broker signups.

| Element | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Visual concept** | Black background; gold headline text; **UI screenshot** (dashboard or AI listing assistant) mocked into device frame or floating card |
| **Headline** | Close more deals with AI |
| **Primary text** | LECIPM helps brokers capture more leads, optimize listings, and close faster using AI. |
| **CTA button** | Book Demo |

**Design notes:** Single hero line in gold; screenshot slightly desaturated so gold text pops; optional subtle vignette.
