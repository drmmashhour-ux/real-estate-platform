# First 100 users — daily execution plan

Realistic system for solo or small-team execution. Adjust numbers to your capacity.

## Every day

| Activity | Target |
|----------|--------|
| Outbound messages to landlords / owners | **30–50** |
| Short-form videos | **2–3** |
| Follow-ups on all replies | **Same day** when possible |
| Listings onboarded | **1–3** |
| Active users / commitments moved forward | **At least 1** meaningful step (call booked, listing received, or paid step agreed) |

## Weekly targets (example)

- **200–300** outbound touches
- **14–21** videos
- **5–15** listings in pipeline
- **3–10** conversations moving to “property link received” or stronger

## Simple KPI tracking

Track in a spreadsheet or **`docs/growth/first-100-users-metrics.md`**:

- Messages sent  
- Replies  
- Listings onboarded  
- Leads generated (your definition)  
- Paid users  
- Revenue  

## If replies are low

- Change the **first line** only (test 3 variants for a week).
- Improve **specificity** (reference their building type, neighborhood, or price band).
- Send at **different times** (evenings often work for small landlords).
- Shorten messages; remove extra links in the first touch.

## If views are high but leads are low

- Tighten the CTA (one DM keyword, one action).
- Add **one** credibility line (process, not fake reviews).
- Drive to **`/get-leads`** or DM — don’t split across three CTAs.
- Revisit **audience**: you may be reaching renters instead of owners.
