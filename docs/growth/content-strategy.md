# LECIPM — Short-form video content strategy

A viral-oriented content system to drive traffic and leads through TikTok and Instagram Reels.

---

## Content pillars

Rotate ideas across these four themes so the feed stays varied and algorithm-friendly:

| Pillar | Examples |
|--------|----------|
| **1. Property insights** | Price vs comps, neighbourhood trends, listing red flags |
| **2. AI analysis** | Deal scores, timing signals, risk flags explained in plain language |
| **3. Investment tips** | Yield, cash flow angles, buyer vs investor framing |
| **4. Broker life / behind the scenes** | Day in the life, objections, “what clients don’t see,” workflow wins |

---

## Video format (required)

**Length:** 15–30 seconds  

**Structure:**

1. **Hook** — first ~2 seconds (pattern interrupt, bold claim, or question)  
2. **Value** — one clear insight or takeaway  
3. **Call to action** — single next step tied to LECIPM  

---

## Script 1 — Deal analysis

**Hook:**  
“This property is overpriced — here’s why”

**Content:**

- Show price vs market (comps or range on screen)  
- Explain one concrete reason fast (location, condition, pricing vs trend)  

**CTA:**  
“Full analysis on LECIPM”

---

## Script 2 — Best deal

**Hook:**  
“Best deal in Montréal right now”

**Content:**

- Highlight one property (photo or listing snippet)  
- One line on why it’s strong value (price/sqft, yield, motivation signal)  

**CTA:**  
“Check it on LECIPM”

---

## Script 3 — AI feature

**Hook:**  
“This AI tells you when to buy real estate”

**Content:**

- Show deal score or AI insight UI (even a mock walkthrough is fine)  
- Tie the number to a decision (“green = strong relative value”)  

**CTA:**  
“Try it on LECIPM”

---

## Script 4 — Broker value

**Hook:**  
“Brokers are losing deals because of this”

**Content:**

- Name the problem (slow follow-up, weak pricing story, scattered tools)  
- Bridge to outcome (speed + clarity wins)  

**CTA:**  
“LECIPM fixes it”

---

## Posting plan

- **Frequency:** 1–2 videos per day  
- **Platforms:** TikTok, Instagram Reels (same asset, platform-native captions where needed)  

Batch film hooks in one session; edit into multiple cuts to sustain daily output.

---

## Optimization

- **Captions on-screen** — many viewers watch muted  
- **Subtitles** — auto-generate then fix names and numbers  
- **Fast pace** — jump cuts, tight sentences, no long intros  
- **Strong thumbnail / first frame** — matches the hook text  

---

## Funnel

```
Video → curiosity → click (bio / link in profile) → LECIPM → lead capture
```

Every video should imply one curiosity gap the product resolves (analysis, score, listings).

---

## Metrics

Track weekly (per platform and per script type):

| Metric | Why it matters |
|--------|----------------|
| **Views** | Reach and hook strength |
| **Clicks** | CTA and curiosity alignment |
| **Leads generated** | Bottom-line ROI; tie UTM or short links to campaigns |

---

## Result

A **scalable growth engine**: repeatable pillars, a fixed 15–30s format, four ready scripts, a realistic posting rhythm, optimization habits, a clear funnel, and metrics that connect content to leads.
