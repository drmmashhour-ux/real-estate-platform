# LECIPM — First 100 users: core offer & positioning

## A. Core offer

**Primary positioning:** We help you get qualified tenants and buyers faster using AI + targeted exposure.

This describes the *intent* of the service (better exposure and lead quality), not a guaranteed number of leads or conversions.

## B. Short value proposition

- Get more serious inquiries (fewer time-wasters).
- Spend less time on low-quality back-and-forth.
- Promote listings faster with a simple, lead-focused flow.
- A clearer path for small landlords and sellers who already list elsewhere.

## C. Simple pricing options

Document these clearly with prospects; adjust if your live checkout differs.

| Option | What it is |
|--------|------------|
| **First listing free** | Onboarding offer: first property promoted at no listing fee so they can see the process. |
| **$15 listing** | A simple paid listing/promotion option when a nominal fee fits the conversation. |
| **$275 per qualified lead** | Pay-per-qualified-lead when you agree on what “qualified” means (e.g. budget fit, timeline, viewing intent). |

**Note:** Align any in-app Stripe amounts with your actual products; this doc is the *sales* framing.

## D. Target user (start narrow)

Pick **one or two** segments first:

1. **Small landlords / hosts** with **1–5** properties who need tenants or short-stay bookings and already feel overwhelmed by messages.
2. **Owners already posting** on Facebook Marketplace, Kijiji, or similar — they have a listing URL and want better-quality interest, not necessarily a full re-platform.

## E. Simple sales language

Use short, credible framing:

- **Results first** — “We can start with your first listing at no listing fee so you can see how we work.”
- **Easy onboarding** — “Send your link or address; we’ll confirm details with you.”
- **Fast listing promotion** — “We focus on getting your property in front of people who are more likely to be serious.”
- **Qualified leads focus** — “We care about fit and intent, not inbox volume.”

Avoid: guaranteed rents, guaranteed sale prices, or “AI will close the deal for you.”
