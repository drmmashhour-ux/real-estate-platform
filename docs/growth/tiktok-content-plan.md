# TikTok / short-form content plan — early leads

## A. Posting plan

- **2–3 short videos per day** while you’re in active acquisition mode.
- Batch-record hooks on one day; schedule or post natively per platform rules.

## B. Video ideas (examples)

- Why your listing is not getting tenants
- This landlord is losing leads (story pattern — anonymized)
- Is this apartment worth the rent? (commentary, not financial advice)
- 3 mistakes that stop you from getting buyers
- How to get qualified tenants faster (process tips)

## C. CTA

**DM me “LISTING”** and I’ll help you get qualified leads.

Keep the CTA identical across videos so replies are easy to track.

## D. Simple video format

1. **Hook** in the first **2 seconds** (question or bold line).
2. **One insight** or story beat (15–30 seconds).
3. **CTA** at the end — DM keyword + what you’ll do next.

## E. 15 sample post ideas (titles / hooks)

1. “Stop answering ‘Is this still available?’ — here’s why.”
2. “3 signs your rental listing is attracting browsers, not tenants.”
3. “You posted on Marketplace — why are the leads so random?”
4. “Landlord mistake #1: copying the same description everyone uses.”
5. “Is your rent too high — or is your lead flow the problem?”
6. “What ‘qualified tenant’ actually means before you waste a Saturday.”
7. “This inbox is costing you hours (and you don’t realize it).”
8. “Before you drop the price — fix this first.”
9. “Why serious renters ghost after the first message.”
10. “Kijiji vs Facebook — where we see the noisiest DMs.”
11. “One thing every seller should put in the first line of the ad.”
12. “How we promote a listing without spamming random groups.”
13. “You don’t need more leads — you need better ones.”
14. “Tenant wanted: budget fit beats ‘interested’ every time.”
15. “Send me ‘LISTING’ — I’ll look at your post and tell you one fix.”

**Compliance:** Avoid guaranteed income, guaranteed tenancy, or investment advice unless licensed and appropriate.
