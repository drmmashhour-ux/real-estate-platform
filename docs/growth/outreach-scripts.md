# Outreach scripts — early conversion

Use as templates; personalize city, platform name, and listing URL.

---

## A. Facebook Marketplace / listing outreach

**Script 1**

Hey! I saw your listing.

Quick question — are you getting enough serious tenants or buyers?

We help property owners get qualified leads using AI + targeted exposure.

I can list it for free and show you results first.

Want me to show you?

---

## B. Follow-up script

Hey, just following up in case you still want help getting more serious leads for your property.

I can help you get it in front of more qualified people this week.

---

## C. Close script

Send me the property link or details and I’ll take care of it.

---

## D. Paid conversion script

I can keep sending qualified leads — it’s **$275 per qualified lead** or **$15 per listing** depending on what works best for you.

(Clarify in writing what “qualified” means before they pay.)

---

## Tone variants

### Short version

Saw your listing — want more serious inquiries? We promote with targeted exposure. First listing free to try. Send your link?

### More professional

I noticed your listing. Many owners see a high volume of low-intent inquiries. We help focus exposure on more relevant prospects using AI-assisted promotion. If you’re open to it, we can start with your first listing at no listing fee so you can evaluate fit.

### More casual

Your place looks solid — are the messages you get actually serious? We help cut the noise and get better-fit leads. Drop the link if you want a hand.
