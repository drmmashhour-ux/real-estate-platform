## 1. Viral content plan (TikTok + Reels)

### Goal

- Drive traffic to Darlink  
- Build trust  
- Make the platform feel “known everywhere”

### Three content types

#### A. Property showcase (highest priority)

- Fast cuts; phone camera is fine  
- **10–20 seconds**

**Angles (examples):**

- “شقة للإيجار في دمشق 🔥”  
- “بيت فخم بسعر صادم 😳”

**Script pattern:**

> 🔥 شقة للإيجار بالمزة  
> سعر مناسب + موقع ممتاز  
>  
> شوف التفاصيل كاملة على دارلينك 👇  
> darlink.com  

#### B. Educational (authority)

Connects to pricing / trust narrative (and later product: AI-assisted pricing insights).

**Examples:**

- “أفضل مناطق للسكن في دمشق”  
- “كم سعر الإيجار الطبيعي؟”  
- “كيف تعرف إذا السعر غالي؟”

#### C. Hook / viral

**Examples:**

- “أرخص شقة في دمشق 😱”  
- “ما رح تصدق السعر…”  
- “ليش هاد العقار فرصة؟”

### Viral formula (every video)

1. **Hook** — first ~2 seconds  
2. **Visual** — property and/or map  
3. **CTA** — Darlink  

### Posting cadence

- **2–3 posts/day**  
- Same asset on **TikTok + Instagram Reels** (reuse)

### CTA (mandatory)

- “شوف الإعلان كامل على دارلينك”  
- “الرابط بالبايو”

## 2. Broker / lister sales strategy

### Goal

- Listings **fast**  
- **Supply** before perfection  

### Reality (Syria)

- Many informal brokers  
- Heavy **Facebook** usage  
- Little structured tooling  

**Positioning:** you win by being **easier**.

### Steps

**1 — Find them**

- Facebook groups  
- Telegram  
- Marketplace  

**2 — First message (Arabic template)**

> مرحبا 👋  
> نحن نطلق منصة جديدة للعقارات في سوريا (دارلينك)  
>  
> حالياً النشر مجاني بالكامل  
> ويمكنك الوصول لعدد أكبر من العملاء بسهولة 🚀  
>  
> هل تحب ننشر لك عقاراتك؟  

**3 — Remove friction**

- “We post for you”  
- “Send photos + price”  
- **You do the work** at the start  

### Power move

- **Featured free for early users** → loyalty + stories  

### Retention (later)

- Show **views**, **leads**, **performance**  
- **Then** introduce paid featured / fees

## 3. Fundraising plan

### When **not** to raise

- Not “idea stage” — raise when there is **traction**.

### Stage 1 — Proof

Targets (illustrative):

- **500–1,000** listings  
- Daily active usage  
- Engagement signals  
- **First revenue**  

### Who to talk to

- Syrian investors  
- Middle East angels  
- Diaspora investors  

### Pitch spine (10 slides)

| Slide | Topic |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | Vision — AI-powered real estate for Syria |
| 2 | Problem — fragmentation, trust, opaque pricing |
| 3 | Solution — Darlink: marketplace + AI + maps |
| 4 | Product — screenshots |
| 5 | Market — scale, weak incumbent |
| 6 | Traction — numbers |
| 7 | Revenue — listings, featured, BNHub |
| 8 | Advantage — AI, maps, first mover |
| 9 | Vision — regional expansion |
| 10 | Ask — funding / partnerships |

### Funding phases

| Phase | Focus |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | Bootstrap, organic growth |
| 2 | Small round (illustrative **$25k–$100k**) |
| 3 | Scale |
