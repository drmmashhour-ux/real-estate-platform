# Listing acquisition — outreach templates

Editable, copy-paste friendly. Adjust tone and city for your market. **Do not** claim you copied or imported someone’s listing from another site.

---

## 1) Owner — direct contact

**Subject:** Get your property in front of serious buyers — with your permission only

Hi {{name}},

We’re building a clearer way for owners to reach qualified buyers on {{platform}}. We don’t copy listings from other sites — we only publish what you (or your agent) approve and provide.

If you’re open to it, we can:
- Add a **short, factual summary** you approve  
- Use **your photos** or new shots you send  
- Route **direct inquiries** to you  

Interested in a 10-minute call?

— {{sender}}

---

## 2) Broker — lead pitch

**Subject:** More qualified exposure for your listings (permission-based)

Hi {{name}},

We work with brokers who want **additional reach** without duplicating MLS or portal content illegally. We publish only **broker-authorized** inventory with **rewritten summaries** and **your** marketing assets.

Happy to show how we onboard listings safely and track intake from first contact to publish.

— {{sender}}

## 3) BNHub — host pitch

Hi {{name}},

{{platform}} helps hosts list short-term stays with verification-friendly onboarding. We **never** pull photos or descriptions from third-party sites; everything is **host-submitted** or **operator-entered with your OK**.

We can help you publish or **add the listing for you** once you send photos and basics.

— {{sender}}

## 4) Follow-up

Hi {{name}},

Following up on listing exposure on {{platform}}. Still the right time? If easier, reply with **city + property type** and we’ll send a short checklist — no obligation.

— {{sender}}

## 5) “We can add it for you”

Hi {{name}},

If you don’t want to use the form, we can **create the draft for you** after a quick call. You’ll confirm **permission**, **photos**, and the **final text** before anything goes live.

— {{sender}}

---

### Positioning reminders

- Prefer: *direct exposure*, *direct leads*, *direct inquiries*, *faster than traditional channels*, *you stay in control*.  
- Avoid: *guaranteed sale*, *we imported your listing from X*, *we copied your listing*.
