## App Name

## Short Description

Book, manage, and track your BNHub stays in one premium guest app.

## Full Description

BNHub Guest helps travelers manage their reservation experience from booking to checkout.

Use the app to:

- view your trips and reservation details
- check booking status and stay information
- access check-in guidance
- receive updates and guest notifications
- message support from inside your booking flow
- track important reservation actions in one place

BNHub Guest is designed to make the guest journey easier, clearer, and more professional, with a premium mobile-first experience connected to the BNHub platform.
