## Privacy Policy Draft

### BNHub Guest Privacy Policy

Effective date: replace with launch date

BNHub Guest is a mobile application operated under LECIPM / BNHub. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the BNHub Guest mobile app and related services available through `https://lecipm.com`.

### Information We Collect

We may collect:

- account information such as your name, email address, and phone number
- booking and reservation information
- messages and support communications sent through the platform
- device and app information needed for security, diagnostics, and notifications
- notification token information when you allow push notifications
- usage data related to app features, reservations, and support interactions

### How We Use Information

We use information to:

- provide reservation and guest account services
- display booking details and trip information
- support check-in, stay, and guest communication flows
- send important service notifications and reservation updates
- improve app performance, stability, and product quality
- detect fraud, misuse, abuse, or unauthorized activity
- comply with legal, contractual, and regulatory obligations

### Notifications

If you allow notifications, we may send service-related alerts such as:

- booking updates
- trip reminders
- check-in related messages
- support or issue-status updates

You can manage notification permissions in your device settings.

### Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed:

- with service providers supporting hosting, messaging, analytics, and infrastructure
- with transaction or booking counterparties where required for the service
- when required by law, legal process, or safety obligations
- during fraud prevention, payment review, dispute handling, or trust and safety review

### Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain internal business records.

### Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect platform and account data. However, no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

### Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, or deletion of certain personal information, subject to legal and operational limitations.
